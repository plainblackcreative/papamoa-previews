Frequently asked
Airport questions
How far is Tauranga Airport from Pāpāmoa? ⌄
Tauranga Airport is approximately 17km from central Pāpāmoa - around 20 minutes by car under normal traffic conditions. The route is via Pāpāmoa Beach Road, Te Okuroa Drive and SH2 North to Jean Batten Drive in Mount Maunganui. Allow extra time during school holidays and summer weekends when SH2 traffic can be heavy.
What airlines fly from Tauranga? ⌄
Air New Zealand is the primary carrier, operating multiple daily flights to Auckland, and daily services to Wellington and Christchurch. Sunair operates regional scheduled services to Whangarei, Gisborne, Napier, Whitianga, Claris and Motiti Island using small piston aircraft. Barrier Air serves Great Barrier Island (Claris) with a Cessna Grand Caravan, a service that commenced December 2022.
Is there parking at Tauranga Airport? ⌄
Yes. Short-term parking is near the terminal building - the first 20 minutes are free, making it suitable for drop-offs and pick-ups. Long-term parking for multi-day trips is available behind the roundabout on entry. All pay stations accept cash, EFTPOS and credit/debit cards. For current daily and hourly rates visit airport.tauranga.govt.nz/parking/parking-rates.
How do I get a shuttle from Pāpāmoa to the airport? ⌄
Two shuttle operators serve Pāpāmoa: Luxury Airport Shuttles (luxuryairportshuttles.co.nz) and Divine Travel and Tours NZ (divinetravel.co.nz). Both offer door-to-door service and should be booked in advance. Two authorised taxi companies also pick up from the arrivals area: Tauranga Mount Taxis and Tauranga Taxi Cabs. The taxi fare from central Pāpāmoa to the airport is typically $30–$45 depending on traffic and exact address.
What are the terminal opening hours? ⌄
The terminal building is open from 5:00am to 9:30pm, seven days a week. Travellers are not able to stay overnight in the terminal. A range of accommodation is available within a five-minute drive of the airport - see the Accommodation section of Papamoa.info for options including holiday parks and motels.
My flight has been cancelled - what do I do? ⌄
Contact your airline directly - not the airport. For Air New Zealand: 0800 737 000 or airnewzealand.co.nz. Tauranga Airport can be affected by fog and low cloud, particularly in winter and early spring. When poor weather is forecast it's worth checking your flight status before leaving Pāpāmoa. The official airport flight board at airport.tauranga.govt.nz/flights shows live status.
