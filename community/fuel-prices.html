Switching to an EV? Find chargers in Pāpāmoa

Pāpāmoa has public EV charging at Excelsa Suburban Centre (Golden Sands) and in the Pāpāmoa Plaza area. The Bay of Plenty’s largest rapid charging hub — 10 bays, up to 300kW — is at Tauranga Crossing, about 18km away.
