🏗 Development Timeline
2021

Bluehaven Group announces The Sands as a billion-dollar town centre development in Pāpāmoa East — described as a ‘once in a generation’ opportunity. Whitiora Medical Centre opens nearby.

❓ Common Questions
What stores are open at The Sands Pāpāmoa? ▼
Stage One of The Sands is open and includes New World The Sands (7am–9pm daily), Anytime Fitness (24/7, the largest in Tauranga), Pablo cafe and restaurant (Latin-inspired, open day and night including late on Friday and Saturday), and Pizza Hut (11am–10pm daily). More stores are opening as Stage Two construction progresses in 2025–2026.
When does Mitre 10 open at The Sands? ▼
Mitre 10 MEGA Pāpāmoa is under construction at The Sands as part of Stage Two. Construction officially began in June 2025, with doors expected to open in 2026. It will be a full Mitre 10 MEGA ‘store of the future’ concept, locally owned and operated.
Where exactly is The Sands Town Centre? ▼
The Sands is on the corner of Stevenson Drive and The Boulevard in Pāpāmoa East, in the Golden Sands residential development. Access is via Te Okuroa Drive. The Pāpāmoa East Interchange connecting State Highway 2 directly to the Golden Sands area is due to open in 2026, which will significantly improve access.
Who is developing The Sands? ▼
The Sands is developed by Bluehaven Group, a Tauranga-based developer with nearly 30 years of experience. The total development is a $1 billion project. On completion it will be four times the size of Bayfair Shopping Centre — 230,000 square metres, equivalent to 50 rugby fields. Already 115,000 people live within 20 minutes of the centre.
What is planned for future stages of The Sands? ▼
Future stages include Mitre 10 MEGA (2026), expanded retail and commercial space, a health hub with primary and secondary care, a $100 million aquatic centre with a 50m Olympic pool, hotels and accommodation, library and civic space, government services and a retirement village. The centre will progressively grow around 11,000 new homes in the Pāpāmoa East area.
Is there parking at The Sands? ▼
Yes, parking at The Sands is free. New World has a dedicated 149-space carpark. Additional surface parking is available throughout the centre. Parking capacity will expand as further stages open.
