🕒 No appointment needed. Just turn up during session times. The service is free. Run by the Bay of Plenty Justice of the Peace Association in partnership with Tauranga City Libraries. Hours can vary on or around public holidays — check the Tauranga City Libraries Facebook page before visiting.

What a JP can help you with

  • Certifying copies of original documents (passport, birth certificate, driver licence, bank statements and more)
  • Witnessing signatures on legal documents and contracts
  • Witnessing and signing statutory declarations and affidavits
  • Signing Citizenship applications and NZ Permanent Residence documents
  • Certifying documents for immigration, legal, financial or government purposes
  • Administering oaths and affirmations

Need a JP outside of these hours? Individual JPs can also be contacted for appointments at their home or workplace. Search the NZ JP Federation locator for JPs in the Pāpāmoa area available at other times.
