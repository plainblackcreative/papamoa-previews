❓ Common Questions
Who installs security systems in Pāpāmoa? ▼
Several companies install security systems in Pāpāmoa. Bay Security Solutions is locally based in Pāpāmoa, owned by David Babbage, and covers residential and commercial jobs across the Bay of Plenty. Bay Security Systems (Andrew Straker) has served the area since 1994 and specialises in residential installations. Security Advantage (Brian) and Armour & Shield Security also serve Pāpāmoa, and larger Tauranga-based companies including Nutech Security and Tarnix cover the area for commercial and monitored security needs.
What types of security systems are available for homes in Pāpāmoa? ▼
Home security options in Pāpāmoa include burglar alarms (including mobile app-connected systems you can arm from your phone), CCTV and security cameras, intercom systems, driveway alert systems, smoke detection and monitored alarms where a 24-hour monitoring centre responds to activations. Most installers offer free no-obligation quotes and can tailor a system to your property.
What is monitored security and do I need it? ▼
Monitored security connects your alarm to a 24-hour monitoring centre. When your alarm activates, the centre contacts you and can dispatch a patrol response if needed. It provides peace of mind when you are away and is often preferred for rental properties, businesses and holiday homes. Tarnix and several other Bay of Plenty providers offer monitoring as an add-on to alarm installation.
Can I control my security system from my phone? ▼
Yes. Most modern alarm and CCTV systems available in Pāpāmoa can be connected to mobile apps, allowing you to arm and disarm your alarm remotely, receive notifications and view camera footage from your phone. Ask your installer specifically about app-connected options when getting a quote.
How much does a home security system cost in Pāpāmoa? ▼
The cost varies depending on the size of your property, the number of alarm zones, whether CCTV is included and whether you add monitoring. Most local installers offer free no-obligation quotes. Contact providers directly for current pricing tailored to your home or business.
Do security systems help reduce home insurance premiums in New Zealand? ▼
In many cases, yes. Some New Zealand insurers offer discounts for monitored alarm systems or verified CCTV installations. Check with your insurer before installation to understand which systems qualify and what documentation they require to apply a discount.
