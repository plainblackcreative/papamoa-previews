🌙 About Dinner at the Domain

Dinner at the Domain is an outdoor evening food and community event held at the Pāpāmoa Domain — the open reserve adjacent to the beach at the western end of the suburb. The event brings together local food vendors, drink options and live music in a relaxed beachside atmosphere. Families spread out on the grass, children play freely, and the Pāpāmoa community gathers in the kind of informal way that only works when the weather is warm and the beach is ten metres away.

The event has become one of the most anticipated dates on Pāpāmoa's social calendar. It typically runs in the evening through the summer season, with multiple events across the season rather than a single annual occasion.

🍽️ Food & Vendors

Each Dinner at the Domain event features a rotating selection of food vendors — street food, gourmet burgers, wood-fired pizza, Asian cuisine, desserts and beverages. Vendors vary by event. There is no set menu — the variety is part of the appeal. Bring cash as not all vendors accept card. Licensed bar typically present.
