Practical guide
💡 EV charging tips for Pāpāmoa visitors
🔐
Download ChargeNet before you arrive
The ChargeNet app (free, iOS and Android) lets you find chargers, check live availability, start a session and pay — all from your phone. Set up your account before your trip. The 24/7 support line is 0800 224 274.
🏭
Use PlugShare to find all networks
PlugShare aggregates all charging networks — ChargeNet, Tesla Destination, free councils chargers, and more — in one map. User reviews flag working vs faulty chargers. Essential for finding free or slow destination chargers at holiday parks and attractions.
⚡
Understand charger speeds
AC (slow/destination): 7–22kW, adds 30–100km/hr. Great for overnight stops.
DC fast: 50–150kW, adds 100–350km/hr. ChargeNet standard.
DC rapid: 150–300kW (Tauranga Crossing), adds 400km+ in 30 min.
🏒
Charge while you shop or eat
The most efficient approach in Pāpāmoa is to charge while doing something else. Plug in at Excelsa or the Plaza, grab groceries or lunch, and leave with more range than when you arrived. Tauranga Crossing's 10-bay hub is designed exactly for this — 30 minutes charges most vehicles significantly.
🏋
AIMS Games & peak season
During the AIMS Games (Sep 2026) and summer holidays, Pāpāmoa sees a big surge in visitors. Charging stations can be busier than usual — plan charges for early morning or late evening, or use the larger Tauranga Crossing hub which has 10 simultaneous bays.
🕐
Be courteous
Move your vehicle once charging is complete — blocking a charging bay when you're done is the single biggest complaint in the NZ EV community. Most ChargeNet stations have an idle fee after your session ends. Don't park in EV bays if you're not charging.
Frequently asked
EV charging questions
Where can I charge my EV in Pāpāmoa? ⌄
The main public EV charging location in Pāpāmoa is at Excelsa Suburban Centre (1194 Pāpāmoa Beach Road, Golden Sands, Pāpāmoa East). ChargeNet also maintains stations in the Pāpāmoa Plaza area — check charge.net.nz/map for current locations and availability. For rapid charging, the Tauranga Crossing ChargeNet hub (Tauriko, ~18km away) is the best option in the region.
Is there a Tesla Supercharger near Pāpāmoa? ⌄
The nearest Tesla Supercharger to Pāpāmoa is in Tauranga. Check the Tesla app or tesla.com for current New Zealand Supercharger locations. Most modern non-Tesla EVs can use the ChargeNet network, and some Tesla vehicles can also use ChargeNet stations with the appropriate adapter.
How do I use a ChargeNet charging station? ⌄
Download the free ChargeNet app (iOS or Android) and create a free account. At the charger, plug in your EV then start a session via the app or by tapping your RFID fob on the charger. Make sure your EV is turned off before plugging in — some vehicles will not begin charging while switched on. ChargeNet's 24/7 phone support is 0800 224 274.
How much does EV charging cost in Pāpāmoa? ⌄
Charging costs vary by network and charger speed. ChargeNet fast chargers typically cost $0.30–$0.60 per kWh — check charge.net.nz or the app for current pricing at specific stations. Some destination chargers at accommodation or retail are free to use. Filter by "Free" on PlugShare to find complimentary chargers near Pāpāmoa.
What is the fastest EV charger near Pāpāmoa? ⌄
The fastest publicly available chargers near Pāpāmoa are at the ChargeNet hub at Tauranga Crossing (Tauriko), featuring 150kW and 300kW chargers capable of adding up to 400km of range in around 30 minutes. This is New Zealand's largest EV charging hub as of 2024 and is about 18km from central Pāpāmoa via SH2.
Can I charge my EV for free in Pāpāmoa? ⌄
Some destination chargers — typically slower AC chargers at accommodation, retail centres and attractions — are free to use. Check the PlugShare app and filter by "Free" to find free chargers near Pāpāmoa. Public rapid chargers (ChargeNet and others) are paid. Holiday parks in Pāpāmoa may also offer EV charging for guests — ask when booking.
