About Pāpāmoa: A coastal suburb of Tauranga, Bay of Plenty, stretching 16 kilometres along the Pacific Ocean. Home to around 37,800 residents and one of New Zealand's fastest-growing communities. Located 15 minutes east of Tauranga city centre and 10 minutes from Mount Maunganui. Known for its white sand beach, family-friendly feel, surf, and as the host area for the Zespri AIMS Games each September. Major retail centres include Pāpāmoa Plaza, Fashion Island, and The Sands at Golden Sands. New development continues at Pāpāmoa East and Te Tumu. Population & stats →
