About Pacific Park Christian Holiday Camp

Pacific Park Christian Holiday Camp has operated near Pāpāmoa Beach since 1944 — making it one of the longest-running holiday venues in the region. Today it holds a 4-star TripAdvisor rating from 321 reviews and is ranked #1 accommodation in Pāpāmoa.

The park is smoke, vape and alcohol-free, making it a popular choice for family groups, school camps and church retreats. Accommodation options range from powered campsites to air-conditioned 2-bed cabins and motel-style units.

A conference centre and group lodges make it suited for larger gatherings. The beachfront location on Pāpāmoa Beach Road is within easy walking distance of the beach and main shopping centres.

What they offer

  • Powered campsites
  • Unserviced sites
  • Air-conditioned 2-bed cabins
  • Serviced motel units
  • Group lodges
  • Conference centre
  • School camp facilities
