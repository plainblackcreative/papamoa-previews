The Pizza Pundits

Food & Drink Pizza Takeaways 📍 Pāpāmoa Beach
★ Gold Spotlight

The Pizza Pundits operates out of a compact shopfront at 1/898 Pāpāmoa Beach Road, turning out gourmet pizzas that have earned them a near-perfect rating on TripAdvisor — currently 4.9 stars from 58 reviews and consistently ranked in the top ten restaurants in Pāpāmoa. Delivery is available through DoorDash and Order Meal, or customers can pick up directly from the store. A genuinely local operation with a loyal following.

Pāpāmoa Orthodontist

Health & Wellbeing Dental Orthodontics 📍 Tara Road, Pāpāmoa
★ Gold Spotlight

Pāpāmoa Orthodontist is a dedicated specialist orthodontic clinic at 1G Tara Road, serving patients from Pāpāmoa, Mount Maunganui, Te Puke and across Tauranga. The clinic was co-founded by a specialist orthodontist and runs alongside Tara Road Dental in the same purpose-built facility, making it a convenient one-stop dental address for local families. They treat children, teenagers and adults, with an emphasis on personalised treatment plans developed after thorough clinical assessment. The practice phrase — "we approach our practice with the care we would give our own family" — reflects a deliberately unhurried, patient-first model. Traditional metal braces, tooth-coloured ceramic braces, and clear aligner options are all available, as are both fixed and removable retainers on completion of treatment. Flexible payment options and transparent comprehensive treatment fees that cover placement, adjustments, retainers and follow-up visits make costs predictable. Free parking is available on site.

Tara Road Dental

Health & Wellbeing Dental General Dentist 📍 Tara Road, Pāpāmoa
★ Gold Spotlight

Tara Road Dental is a modern, purpose-built dental practice at 1G Tara Road, Pāpāmoa, co-founded by Dr Lloyd Farrar and his wife Rachel (who operates the adjacent Pāpāmoa Orthodontist clinic). The practice is built around changing how patients feel about going to the dentist — with a deliberately calm environment, attentive team and a frank communication style that avoids clinical jargon. Dr Farrar trained at the University of Otago and has worked in hospital and general dental settings across New Zealand and internationally, bringing particular expertise in intravenous sedation, wisdom tooth surgery and dental implants. Daily ACC appointments are available for dental injury. IV sedation using Midazolam is offered for patients who need support through longer or more complex procedures, giving anxious patients a safe, comfortable option. The practice is also a participant in the TerraCycle Waste Brigade, diverting used oral care products from landfill. Conveniently located a short drive from Te Puke, Mount Maunganui and central Tauranga.

Pacific Park Christian Holiday Camp

Accommodation Holiday Parks Group Retreats 📍 1110 Pāpāmoa Beach Road
★ Gold Spotlight

Pacific Park Christian Holiday Camp has been a fixture on Pāpāmoa Beach Road since 1944, when it was founded as Mount Bible Class Camp in response to the founders' concerns about the influence of summer holiday culture on young people. Today it operates as a fully equipped, family-friendly holiday park just a two-minute walk from Pāpāmoa Beach, ranked number one in Pāpāmoa on TripAdvisor with 321 reviews and a 4/5 rating. The park runs entirely without alcohol, smoking or vaping — a draw for families wanting a genuinely quiet environment, and no barrier for non-religious guests (many reviewers specifically mention this). Accommodation spans powered campsites, unserviced cabins, air-conditioned two-bedroom cabins, fully serviced motel units and larger self-contained lodges for groups. Facilities include a hot tub, laundry, communal kitchens, BBQ areas, a jumping pillow, children's playground, games room, tennis, and a conference centre. As a non-profit, the park actively supports community ministries and charities and offers ministry discounts. Office hours are 8:30am–6:30pm seven days, with late arrivals accommodated on request.

Karaka Pines Pāpāmoa

Retirement Living Independent Living Lifestyle Village 📍 Te Ara O Wairakei walkway, Pāpāmoa
★ Gold Spotlight

Karaka Pines Pāpāmoa is the eighth village in the Karaka Pines group and the newest retirement community in the area, positioned alongside the Te Ara O Wairakei walkway with a direct footpath connecting residents to Pāpāmoa Beach Road. The project is planned for 114 units across a mix of two-bedroom duplexes and three-bedroom standalone homes, all designed for independent living with generous open-plan layouts and private outdoor spaces. Karaka Pines' defining commercial feature is its full capital gains model: residents share in the future value of their home when they sell, rather than the village operator retaining the gain — a structure the company says is unique at scale in New Zealand retirement living. A clubhouse is planned for the village with shared dining, lounge, bar, library, games and hobby rooms, gym and wellness centre. The Sands Town Centre is minutes away by foot or car, giving easy access to supermarkets, cafés, medical services and boutique retail. Homes start from $875,000. ReDefined Wellbeing Hub

Health & Wellbeing Gym & Fitness Wellness 📍 58 Ashley Place, Pāpāmoa
★ Gold Spotlight

ReDefined Wellbeing Hub describes itself as New Zealand's first fully integrated wellbeing centre, operating from 58 Ashley Place in Pāpāmoa Beach. Founded by Jimmy August — who began his career as a Les Mills personal trainer in Wellington and later moved into health club management in London — ReDefined brings together movement, health services and holistic practitioners under a single roof. The facility runs three dedicated studios: Performance (strength training), MindBody (yoga, hot yoga, Pilates), and Movement (Reformer Pilates), alongside an open gym, and a new Recovery Lab featuring two saunas, compression therapy and cold plunge pools at 12–4 degrees Celsius. Health practitioners on site include registered clinical nutritionists, naturopaths, a lifestyle and integrative medicine GP, and remedial manual therapists. DNA testing (Health, Diet and Sport profiles) is offered through Kylie, the on-site registered clinical nutritionist and naturopath. ReDefined also offers a Reformer Pilates programme for pregnant members and is a partner with Pāpāmoa FC. The ethos draws on Blue Zones longevity research and frames recovery as equally important to training. Bookings via the ReDefined app or at reception.
