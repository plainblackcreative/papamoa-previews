About The Pizza Pundits

The Pizza Pundits is a gourmet pizza kitchen at 1/898 Pāpāmoa Beach Road, open seven nights a week from 4:30pm. They hold a 4.9-star rating from 58 TripAdvisor reviews and consistently place in Pāpāmoa’s top ten restaurants — a strong record for a takeaway-focused operation.

The kitchen makes its own gluten-free bases from raw ingredients rather than buying them pre-made — a genuine point of difference that customers specifically call out in reviews. The signature Narnia pizza draws returning customers. The menu covers classic combinations, vegetarian options and more inventive specialty pizzas. Pricing sits in the mid-range.

Delivery is available through DoorDash, Uber Eats and Delivereasy. The owners are hands-on and community-minded, running youth sports voucher programmes for several local Pāpāmoa clubs.
