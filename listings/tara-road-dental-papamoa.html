About Tara Road Dental

Tara Road Dental is a purpose-built dental clinic at 1G Tara Road, Pāpāmoa, designed from the ground up to feel calm and welcoming. The team takes a compassionate approach to care -- whether a patient is coming in for a routine check-up or a more complex procedure, the goal is a positive, reassuring experience. For patients who feel nervous about the dentist, that focus on comfort makes a genuine difference.

The practice offers a full range of dental services: general and preventative dentistry, dental hygienist appointments, cosmetic treatments (veneers, whitening, crowns, bridges), dental implants, wisdom tooth removal and root canal treatment. IV sedation using Midazolam is available for anxious patients or longer procedures -- most patients describe it as a restful sleep with no memory of the treatment. Daily ACC appointments are available for dental injuries.

The team also places strong emphasis on prevention -- regular hygienist visits, personalised home care advice, and guidance on diet and daily routines that protect teeth and gums long-term. Gum health is linked to broader health conditions including cardiovascular disease, and the practice treats it as an important part of overall wellbeing. Tara Road Dental is conveniently located for patients travelling from Te Puke, Mount Maunganui and Tauranga.

Frequently asked questions
Does Tara Road Dental offer IV sedation? ▼
Yes. IV sedation using Midazolam is available for patients with dental anxiety or those undergoing longer or more complex procedures. Most patients describe the experience as a restful sleep with little or no memory of the treatment. The practice follows DCNZ guidelines, and the dentist is trained with the New Zealand Society for Sedation in Dentistry. Speak with the team to discuss whether sedation is suitable for your situation.
Is Tara Road Dental suitable for nervous patients? ▼
Yes. The practice was designed with anxious patients in mind -- the clinic environment is calm and welcoming, and the team take time to listen and explain each step before proceeding. For patients with significant dental anxiety or those facing longer procedures, IV sedation (Midazolam) is available. Most patients describe sedation appointments as feeling like a restful sleep, with little or no memory of the treatment. Call 07 579 5291 to discuss your concerns before booking.
Does Tara Road Dental accept ACC appointments? ▼
Yes. Tara Road Dental provides daily ACC appointments for dental injuries. If you have had an accident affecting your teeth, call the practice on 07 579 5291 to arrange a prompt appointment.
What dental implant services are available? ▼
Tara Road Dental offers dental implants to replace one or more missing teeth, providing a secure, natural-looking result that is the closest option to a natural tooth. The team can also discuss alternative options such as dental bridges to find the best outcome for individual needs.
Can Tara Road Dental remove wisdom teeth? ▼
Yes. Wisdom teeth can cause pain, infection or alignment problems if they grow at the wrong angle or become impacted. The team at Tara Road Dental carries out a thorough assessment to determine whether removal is the right step. IV sedation is available for wisdom tooth procedures where needed.
How often should I visit the dental hygienist? ▼
Tara Road Dental recommends visiting the hygienist every six months. A hygienist removes plaque and tartar, monitors gum health and provides a personalised plan for home care. Regular hygiene appointments prevent minor issues from becoming costly treatments, and there is a well-established link between gum health and broader health risks including cardiovascular conditions.
Where is Tara Road Dental located in Pāpāmoa? ▼
Tara Road Dental is at 1G Tara Road, Pāpāmoa Beach. The practice is a short drive from Te Puke, Mount Maunganui and Tauranga. It shares a purpose-built building with Pāpāmoa Orthodontist, though the two clinics operate independently. On-site parking is available.
