About The Cave Pāpāmoa

The Cave is a high-tech entertainment studio at 7/16 Ashley Place in Pāpāmoa, offering hosted virtual reality sessions, VR escape rooms, sim racing simulators and PC gaming. It is one of three Cave locations in New Zealand — alongside Taupō and Palmerston North — and positions itself as a premium indoor activity for families, groups, date nights and corporate teams.

VR sessions are hosted rather than self-directed: a staff member guides participants through experiences suited to their age and comfort level. The Pāpāmoa location runs 12 HTC Vive Pro systems with high-resolution displays and spacious play areas for active gameplay. The library covers hundreds of titles across sport, RPG, sci-fi, arcade and shooters. The sim racing setup uses custom-built rigs with rally, circuit, formula one, go-kart and demolition derby formats — head-to-head racing between participants is popular for groups.

Birthday parties are a core part of the operation. The Cave handles setup, entertainment and a group photo, and provides coffee for parents. Minimum age is 8 years, minimum group size 4 for birthday packages. Corporate groups of up to 40 are catered for. Advance booking is strongly recommended — the venue books out regularly, particularly on weekends and during school holidays.

Frequently asked questions
What is the minimum age for The Cave? ▼
The minimum age of entry is 8 years. This applies to all experiences including VR, sim racing and PC gaming. Birthday party packages require a minimum group size of 4 players aged 8 or over.
Do I need to book in advance? ▼
Yes. Advance booking is strongly recommended. The Cave books out regularly, particularly on weekends and during school holidays. Book online at thecave.nz or call 0800 228 369 to check availability.
Is VR suitable for first-timers and non-gamers? ▼
Yes. All VR sessions are hosted — a staff member guides each guest through the experience, selecting titles that suit their age, comfort level and interests. The Cave caters for first-timers, non-gamers and experienced players alike.
Can The Cave cater for large groups and corporate events? ▼
Yes. The Cave caters for corporate groups of up to 40. They offer customisable team building packages including VR, sim racing and catering options. Contact them directly via thecave.nz for corporate and group bookings.
Do they offer gift vouchers? ▼
Yes. Gift vouchers are available for all experiences. They can be purchased online at thecave.nz and are valid across all three New Zealand Cave locations — Pāpāmoa, Taupō and Palmerston North.
