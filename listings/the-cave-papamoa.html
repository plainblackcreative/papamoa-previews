About The Cave Pāpāmoa

The Cave is a high-tech entertainment studio at 16 Ashley Place in Pāpāmoa, offering hosted virtual reality sessions, VR escape rooms, sim racing simulators and PC gaming. It is one of three Cave locations in New Zealand — alongside Taupō and Palmerston North — and positions itself as a premium indoor activity for families, groups, date nights and corporate teams.

The VR sessions are hosted rather than self-directed, meaning a staff member guides participants through experiences suited to their age and comfort level. The library runs to hundreds of titles across genres including sport, RPG, sci-fi, arcade and shooters. The sim racing setup uses custom-built rigs and covers rally, circuit, formula one and go-kart formats. Head-to-head racing between participants is possible and popular for groups.

Birthday parties are a core part of the operation. The Cave handles the setup, entertainment and group photo, and provides coffee for accompanying parents. Minimum age is 8 years, minimum group size 4 for birthday packages. The venue can cater for corporate groups up to 40 people with a range of entertainment and catering options. Advance booking is strongly recommended — the venue books out regularly, particularly on weekends and during school holidays.
