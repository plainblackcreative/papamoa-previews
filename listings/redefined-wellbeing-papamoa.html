About ReDefined Wellbeing Hub

ReDefined Wellbeing Hub at 58 Ashley Place describes itself as New Zealand’s first fully integrated wellbeing centre. Founded by Jimmy August - who trained as a Les Mills personal trainer in Wellington before moving into health club management in London - ReDefined brings movement, specialist health practitioners and recovery technology together under a single roof.

Three dedicated studios run under one membership: Performance (strength and functional training), MindBody (reformer Pilates, yoga and hot yoga) and Movement (group fitness). A Recovery Lab features two saunas, a cold plunge operating at 12–4°C, and compression therapy. The design draws on Blue Zones longevity research, treating recovery as equally important to training.

Allied health practitioners operate on site including physiotherapy through Recharge Physio (ACC-registered), clinical nutrition, DNA health testing and an integrative medicine GP. Classes are booked through the ReDefined app, which shows live availability across all studios.
