About PAP House

PAP House is a restaurant and bar on Pāpāmoa Beach Road, positioned as the local’s living room. Open seven days with both indoor and outdoor seating, and a dog-friendly policy that sets it apart from most competing venues in the area.

The menu leans toward casual dining — crowd-pleasing dishes done consistently well, with a strong drinks list covering craft beer, wine and cocktails. The outdoor area fills from late afternoon in summer with the after-beach crowd. It is a reliable choice for a relaxed group dinner, post-surf drinks or a weeknight meal without travelling to Mount Maunganui.

PAP House sits in the affordable to mid-range bracket. The kitchen runs lunch from midday and dinner service through the evening. Group bookings are taken — check Facebook or call ahead for busy periods.
