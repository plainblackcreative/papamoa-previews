About Karaka Pines Pāpāmoa

Karaka Pines Pāpāmoa is the eighth village in the Karaka Pines group and the newest retirement community in the area, positioned alongside the Te Ara O Wairakei walkway with a direct footpath connecting residents to Pāpāmoa Beach Road.

The village is planned for 114 units across a mix of two-bedroom duplexes and three-bedroom standalone homes. Karaka Pines operates a full capital gains model, meaning residents retain 100% of any increase in their unit’s value.

A fixed fees for life option is available, giving residents certainty over ongoing costs. The planned clubhouse, gym and wellness centre are expected to be completed as the village reaches capacity.
