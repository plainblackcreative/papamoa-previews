About Blackberry Eatery

Blackberry Eatery is an all-day breakfast and brunch cafe in Pāpāmoa, known locally for generous portions and good coffee. It is a consistently busy spot on weekends, which speaks to its standing with the local population.

The menu covers the standard brunch range — eggs multiple ways, toast variations, smoothie bowls and cabinet food — with rotating specials. The coffee is a step above the average suburban cafe.

Blackberry Eatery fills a reliable neighbourhood role: somewhere to take the family on a Saturday morning without a reservation and come away satisfied.
