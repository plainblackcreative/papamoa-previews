About Coastal Autos

Coastal Autos has operated from 8 Ashley Place since 2017, building a loyal local following on the back of straightforward communication and consistent workmanship. The workshop handles all makes and models — passenger cars, light commercials, 4WDs, 4-tonne trucks, quad bikes and trailers — and carries MTA Assured status, meaning it operates under the Motor Trade Association’s code of ethics and dispute resolution framework.

Owner Brett and the team are regularly described in reviews as honest about what work is actually needed versus what can wait — a quality that keeps regulars coming back. The workshop also advises customers when a vehicle may not be worth repairing and can help find a replacement if needed. All work carries a 12 month/20,000km warranty on parts and labour, and warranty-approved oils and parts are used throughout, preserving a new car manufacturer’s warranty after servicing.

Online booking is available through the website. Coastal Autos also handles fleet servicing, making it a practical option for local businesses running multiple vehicles.

Frequently asked questions
Is Coastal Autos MTA Approved? ▼
Yes. Coastal Autos holds MTA Assured status with the Motor Trade Association — the largest industry body of its type in New Zealand, representing over 3,600 professionals. MTA membership means the workshop operates under a code of ethics and has access to the MTA’s dispute resolution process, providing an additional layer of consumer protection beyond the standard Consumer Guarantees Act.
Does Coastal Autos offer a warranty on their work? ▼
Yes. All work carries a 12 month/20,000km warranty on parts and labour (some conditions apply). Coastal Autos only uses high-quality, warranty-approved oils and parts, which means a new car’s manufacturer warranty is retained after servicing.
Can I book online? ▼
Yes. Online bookings are available at coastalautos.nz/bookonline. You can also call 07 282 7100 or email info@coastalautos.nz to book or request a quote.
Do they service all vehicle types? ▼
Yes. Coastal Autos services all makes and models including passenger cars, light commercial vehicles, 4WDs, 4-tonne trucks, quad bikes and trailers. Fleet servicing is also available for businesses.
Where is Coastal Autos located? ▼
Coastal Autos is at 8 Ashley Place, Pāpāmoa Beach 3118. Customers regularly come from Mt Maunganui, Te Puke, Bethlehem and the broader Tauranga area.
