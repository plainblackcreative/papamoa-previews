About The Wagon

The Wagon operates from Unit 1, 8 Toorea Street in the Pāpāmoa Junction commercial precinct — the mixed-use development off Te Okuroa Drive that has attracted a growing cluster of food, retail and trade businesses on the eastern edge of Pāpāmoa. The venue holds an on-licence issued by the Tauranga District Licensing Committee.

The kitchen focuses on gourmet burgers made with quality meat, locally sourced vegetables, house-made sauces and freshly baked brioche buns. Signature options include The Wagon Wheel (double beef patty, American cheese, pickles, Wagon sauce) and the Smoky BBQ (beef patty, streaky bacon, smoked cheddar, BBQ glaze, crispy onion rings). Gluten-free options are available. The drinks list covers craft cocktails, beer and wine.

The Toorea Street location has good vehicle access and ample parking, serving both local residents in Pāpāmoa East and those travelling through on State Highway 2. Pickup and delivery are available through Order Meal, with typical hours running Tuesday to Sunday from 11am to 9pm.

Frequently asked questions
Does The Wagon have gluten-free options? ▼
Yes. Gluten-free options are available on the menu. The Wagon Wheel and other burgers can be ordered with GF modifications. Check with the kitchen when ordering if you have a severe intolerance.
Can I order delivery from The Wagon? ▼
Yes. The Wagon is available for delivery and pickup through Order Meal at ordermeal.co.nz/the-wagon-papamoa. Typical hours are Tuesday to Sunday from 11am to 9pm.
Is The Wagon licensed? ▼
Yes. The Wagon holds an on-licence issued by the Tauranga District Licensing Committee, meaning they can serve alcohol on the premises. The drinks list includes craft cocktails, beer and wine.
Where is The Wagon located in Pāpāmoa? ▼
The Wagon is at 8 Toorea Street in the Pāpāmoa Junction precinct — the commercial development off Te Okuroa Drive near State Highway 2. There is ample free parking on site.
What are The Wagon's most popular burgers? ▼
The Wagon Wheel (double beef patty, American cheese, pickles, lettuce, tomato and Wagon sauce on a brioche bun, $22.50) and the Smoky BBQ (beef patty, streaky bacon, smoked cheddar, BBQ glaze and crispy onion rings, $23.50) are the signature options.
