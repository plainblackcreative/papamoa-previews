Known for its commitment to fresh ingredients and bold flavour, The Wagon serves burgers made the right way. Each burger is prepared using quality meat, crisp locally sourced vegetables, signature house-made sauces, and soft, freshly baked buns that bring everything together. The focus is on real food and great taste.

The menu features a range of burgers to suit every preference, from timeless favourites to creative flavour combinations. Diners can also enjoy craft cocktails alongside their meal while relaxing in the friendly, easy-going atmosphere. Whether dining with friends, catching up with family, or grabbing a casual bite, The Wagon is a great spot to experience the growing food scene in Pāpāmoa.
