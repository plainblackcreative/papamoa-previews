Pāpāmoa Orthodontist delivers tailored orthodontic care for children, teens, and adults at their purpose-built clinic on Tara Road. The practice focuses on personalised treatment plans designed to achieve healthy, confident smiles using modern techniques and proven orthodontic technology. From the first consultation through to final retainers, patients receive clear guidance and supportive care at every stage.

Services include traditional braces and clear aligner options, providing solutions for a wide range of alignment and bite concerns. Early assessments are also available for younger patients to determine the best timing for treatment. With flexible payment options and a welcoming environment, the practice makes orthodontic care straightforward and accessible for local families across Pāpāmoa, Mount Maunganui, Te Puke, and the wider Tauranga area.

The experienced team is passionate about oral health and dental wellbeing, delivering high-quality orthodontic care in a relaxed and welcoming environment. With a strong focus on precision and patient satisfaction, they work alongside each patient to achieve a smile they can feel truly confident about. The clinic premises are fully accessible with free parking on site.
