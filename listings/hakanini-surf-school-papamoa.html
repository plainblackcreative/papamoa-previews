About Hakanini Surf School & Rentals

Hakanini Surf School & Rentals operates from the Pāpāmoa Domain, directly adjacent to one of the Bay of Plenty’s most consistent beach breaks. The school offers group and private lessons for all ages and ability levels — from first-timers to improvers working on their pop-up and wave selection.

Holiday programmes run during all school breaks, making it a popular option for families with children. Board hire is available from $35 per session, covering foam boards and shortboards suited to learners at various stages.

Instructors are Pāpāmoa locals with in-depth knowledge of the beach break’s seasonal conditions and rip patterns — a meaningful advantage over instructors unfamiliar with the specific break. Bookings are accepted online and by phone. Advance booking is strongly recommended during school holidays and summer.

Frequently asked questions
How much does surf school cost at Hakanini? ▼
Board hire starts from $35 per session. Group lesson and private lesson pricing is available by contacting Hakanini directly or via their booking page. Holiday programme rates are also available.
Is Hakanini suitable for complete beginners? ▼
Yes. Hakanini runs beginner programmes specifically designed for people with no surfing experience. Foam boards are provided for learner sessions and instructors focus on the pop-up technique and wave reading basics.
Where does Hakanini operate from? ▼
Hakanini operates from the Pāpāmoa Domain, directly adjacent to one of the Bay of Plenty’s most consistent beach breaks. The Domain has parking and toilet facilities nearby.
Do Hakanini run school holiday programmes? ▼
Yes. Holiday programmes run during all school breaks and are structured multi-session programmes aimed at children and teenagers. These book quickly — advance registration is recommended.
How do I book a surf lesson? ▼
Bookings are taken online and by phone. Book in advance during school holidays and summer as sessions fill quickly. Walk-in board hire may be available subject to equipment availability.
