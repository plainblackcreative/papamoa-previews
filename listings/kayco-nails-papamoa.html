About Kayco Nails

Kayco Nails is a small home-based business in Pāpāmoa creating custom press-on nail kits by hand. Each set is made to order — sized and designed specifically for the customer rather than pulled from a shelf. Orders come in through Instagram, where the feed also doubles as a portfolio showing the styles and nail art on offer.

A well-fitted custom set, properly applied, can last one to two weeks and offers a convenient, damage-free alternative to salon acrylics or gels. Because every kit is sized individually, the fit is far better than anything generic. Kits are shipped anywhere in New Zealand.

Check the Instagram feed for current styles, turnaround times and pricing, then send a DM to place an order.
