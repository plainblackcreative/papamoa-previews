About Speedy Screens Pāpāmoa

Speedy Screens is a Pāpāmoa-based business run by owner-operator Rob, who brings over 16 years of experience in the joinery and screen industry. The business specialises in three distinct product lines: custom insect screens, security doors and windows, and built-in wardrobe solutions. All products are made to measure rather than off-the-shelf, which means they can handle non-standard openings and unusual configurations that generic screen products cannot.

Rob handles all consultations, measuring and installation personally rather than subcontracting the work out. The process starts with a free on-site visit where he measures up and provides a quote. Products are then manufactured and professionally installed. The “speedy” in the name refers to turnaround time rather than rushing the work — the focus is on getting jobs scheduled and completed efficiently without compromising on fit or finish.

Speedy Screens is a mobile service with no shopfront or showroom. All consultations happen at the customer’s property. They supply and install Amplimesh insect screen products and Supascreen security mesh systems. The service area covers the entire Bay of Plenty from Waihi through to Waihau Bay, including Pāpāmoa, Tauranga, Mount Maunganui and Te Puke.

Frequently asked questions
What types of insect screens does Speedy Screens install? ▼
Speedy Screens supplies and installs retractable pleated screens, sliding screens, fixed mesh screens and magnetic screens for both doors and windows. All products are custom measured for each opening, so they work with non-standard sizes and shapes. Retractable options fold away when not in use, which suits modern bifold and stacker door setups well.
Does Speedy Screens offer repair and remeshing? ▼
Yes. Speedy Screens offers repair and remeshing services for damaged or worn screens. This can extend the life of existing frames without the cost of a full replacement, and is a practical option for older homes where the frame is still in good condition but the mesh has torn or degraded.
What area does Speedy Screens service? ▼
Their service area covers the entire Bay of Plenty from Waihi in the west through to Waihau Bay in the east, including Pāpāmoa, Tauranga, Mount Maunganui, Te Puke, Whakatāne and Ōpōtiki. For more distant locations it may take longer to schedule a visit, but they do service rural and remote properties across the region.
What does the wardrobe service include? ▼
Speedy Screens builds custom wardrobe interiors with sliding doors, shelving, drawers and fittings including pull-out shoe racks. They build wall-hung and floor-to-ceiling configurations, typically for new builds or renovation projects.
Is there a showroom to visit? ▼
No. Speedy Screens is a mobile service with no shopfront or showroom. All consultations take place at the customer’s property. Rob comes out, measures up and provides a quote on the spot. Call 021 150 1566 or email sales@speedyscreens.co.nz to arrange a free visit.
