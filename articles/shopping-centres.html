Fashion Island

Pāpāmoa Beach Road, Pāpāmoa Beach (western end)

🏬 Major centre

Fashion Island is Pāpāmoa's established western retail hub, anchored by the New World supermarket and housing the suburb's highest concentration of specialty retail. Located on Pāpāmoa Beach Road near the Domain Road intersection, it serves the western end of Pāpāmoa and connecting traffic from Mount Maunganui.

Palm Beach Plaza

Pāpāmoa Beach Road, central Pāpāmoa

🛍️ Strip centre

Palm Beach Plaza is a mid-suburb retail strip on Pāpāmoa Beach Road serving the central residential areas. A mix of services, food and convenience retail positioned between Fashion Island and The Sands, making it a go-to for day-to-day needs without travelling to either end of the suburb.

The Sands Town Centre Newer

Te Okuroa Drive, Pāpāmoa East

✨ Town centre

The Sands is Pāpāmoa East's growing town centre, purpose-built to serve the rapidly expanding eastern residential areas. Located on Te Okuroa Drive off State Highway 2, it is the commercial hub for thousands of new homes in the Pāpāmoa East development. The centre continues to grow as new retailers and services are added.
