🏪 About The Yard

The Yard is a precinct-style retail and dining destination in Pāpāmoa that has developed a loyal following for its curated mix of independent businesses. Unlike the big-box shopping centres, The Yard draws people in with character — smaller, owner-operated businesses in a setting that encourages browsing and lingering.

The precinct is home to a mix of café, food, artisan retail and services. It attracts both locals who visit regularly and visitors who discover it while staying at Pāpāmoa Beach.
