NZ Easter Trading Rules

New Zealand law restricts trading on some Easter holidays. As a general guide:

  • Most shops are required to stay closed on Good Friday and until 1pm on Easter Sunday (some exemptions apply)
  • Easter Saturday is a normal trading day with no restrictions
  • Easter Monday is a public holiday but many businesses choose to open
  • Pharmacies, petrol stations, dairies, cafes and some hospitality venues may trade with restrictions
