Pāpāmoa Library

Free books, internet, children's programmes, printing and community meeting space — Pāpāmoa's public library is a central part of the suburb's community infrastructure.

📚 Services

📖
Books & DVDsFree borrowing for members. New and popular titles, large print and more.
🌐
Free WiFiAvailable throughout the library for visitors without needing a membership.
🖥️
Public computersInternet access available — book a session at the desk or walk in.
🖨️
Printing & scanningBlack and white and colour printing. Scanning and photocopying. Small fee per page.
👶
Children's programmesStory time, school holiday programmes and early literacy activities. Check the library's events calendar.
🏢
Community roomMeeting space available for community groups. Book through TCC facilities.
📰
Digital resourcesAccess to eBooks, audiobooks, newspapers and databases with a library card.
🆓
Free membershipAll Tauranga residents can join free. Bring ID and proof of address.
