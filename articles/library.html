Pāpāmoa Library

Te Ao Mārama — Pāpāmoa Library. Free books, internet access, children's programmes, digital resources, JP visits and community meeting space. Open seven days.

📚 Services

📖
Books, DVDs & audiobooksFree borrowing for members. New releases, popular titles, large print, Te Reo Māori collection and more.
🌐
Free WiFiAvailable throughout the library. No membership required — available to all visitors.
🖥️
Public computersInternet access available. Book a session at the service desk or walk in.
🖨️
Printing & scanningBlack and white and colour printing. Scanning and photocopying available. Small charge per page.
👶
Children's programmesStory time, school holiday programmes, early literacy activities and more. See the events calendar for upcoming sessions.
📱
Digital resourceseBooks and audiobooks (BorrowBox, hoopla), movie streaming (Beamafilm), e-magazines (PressReader), LinkedIn Learning and more — all free with a library card.
⚖️
Justice of the Peace visitsJP services available at the library on a regular schedule. Check library.tauranga.govt.nz for current dates and times.
🏢
Community spacesMeeting and community spaces available for groups. Book through the library — see community spaces page.
📶
Skinny JumpSubsidised home broadband for eligible households. Library staff can help with applications. Free drop-in digital help sessions also available.
🆓
Free membershipAll Tauranga City residents can join free. Bring ID and proof of address, or register online.
