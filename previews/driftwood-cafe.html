About

The Driftwood Café sits just back from Pāpāmoa Beach on Parton Road, drawing a loyal local crowd for its relaxed coastal feel and genuinely good coffee. The team roasts their own single-origin beans on-site — a rarity in the eastern suburbs — and the all-day menu runs until mid-afternoon, with seasonal cabinet food refreshed daily using Bay of Plenty produce.

Dog-friendly outdoor seating, a kids menu, and high chairs make it a natural stop for families. Gluten-free and dairy-free options are always available. Walk-ins welcome; groups of six or more can reserve the back deck by calling ahead.
