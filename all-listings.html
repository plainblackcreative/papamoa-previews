Barber Toms
Neighbourhood barbershop in Pāpāmoa and Mount Maunganui
80 Parton Road, Pāpāmoa · Also at 1 Newton Street, Mt Maunganui
★ Silver
Barber Toms is a walk-in-friendly barbershop on Parton Road that has built a solid following around straightforward service and a relaxed atmosphere. They handle men's and kids' cuts, beard trims, razor fades, and a full "The Works" package that covers everything in one sitting. Bookings are taken through Fresha, and they also accept walk-ins when chairs are free. A second location recently opened on Newton Street in Mount Maunganui. Worth knowing: this is a barber rather than a unisex salon — service is focused on men's and boys' cuts.

ReDefined Wellbeing Hub
Award-winning gym, pilates studio, and recovery centre in Pāpāmoa
Pāpāmoa · Also in Takapuna, Auckland · Exercise NZ Award Winner
★ Gold Spotlight
ReDefined houses five specialised spaces under one roof: a reformer pilates studio, yoga and meditation room, functional strength and conditioning area, open gym, and dedicated recovery lab. On-site health practitioners cover nutrition, physiotherapy, massage, and counselling. They won the Exercise New Zealand Supreme Facility of the Year award in 2023. Membership is structured as a single pass covering all studios and classes. The atmosphere leans community-driven rather than hardcore gym culture — built for long-term habits rather than short-term intensity.

Blackberry Eatery
All-day café and evening dining at Fashion Island, Pāpāmoa
Shop 1/42 Gravatt Road, Fashion Island · 07 572 1724
★ Silver
Blackberry Eatery runs a split-format operation at Fashion Island: café-style breakfast and lunch during the day, then a more substantial dinner service Thursday through Sunday evenings. The coffee programme is built around Allpress beans. The dinner menu is a genuine sit-down dining experience — previous menus have featured lamb shanks, pasta, and dumplings alongside standard evening fare, with handcrafted cocktails and a wine list. Functions are a core part of the business: capacity for up to 120 people, with buffet, cocktail, and working lunch formats.

Hakanini Surf School & Rentals
Pāpāmoa's surf school at the Domain, lessons and board hire for all ages
Pāpāmoa Domain, 559 Pāpāmoa Beach Road · Lessons from $70
★ Gold Spotlight
Hakanini is Pāpāmoa's dedicated surf school, operating from the Domain at the heart of the beach. Founded by Tom Botting, who brings international experience from surfing big waves in Pichilemu, Chile. They run group lessons for up to eight people, private one-on-one sessions, and school holiday programmes. Board hire is available at $35 half-day or $45 full day — one of the more affordable rental options in the Bay of Plenty. Reviews consistently mention the instructors' ability to build confidence in kids and beginners. Booking early is recommended for school holiday programmes.
