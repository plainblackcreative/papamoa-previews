ReDefined Wellbeing Hub

🏋 Integrated Wellbeing · 58 Ashley Place, Pāpāmoa

New Zealand’s first fully integrated wellbeing centre — movement, recovery and allied health under one roof at 58 Ashley Place, Pāpāmoa. Three dedicated studios run under one membership: Performance (strength and functional training), MindBody (reformer Pilates, yoga and hot yoga) and Movement (group fitness). A Recovery Lab features two saunas, a cold plunge at 12–4°C, and compression therapy. Allied health practitioners on site include physiotherapy, clinical nutrition, DNA health testing and an integrative medicine GP.

❓ Common Questions
What gyms are in Pāpāmoa? ▼
Pāpāmoa has over ten gyms and fitness facilities. Options include ReDefined Wellbeing Hub (integrated wellbeing with Pilates, yoga and recovery), Absolute Weights & Fitness (locally owned 24/7), Snap Fitness at Fashion Island (24/7 with global access), Flex Fitness on Tara Road (24/7, Les Mills classes), Bout Hybrid (hybrid training and Hyrox club), CrossFit Papamoa and CrossFit Alliance (two affiliated CrossFit boxes), Oceanblue Health & Fitness at Papamoa Plaza, plus Anytime Fitness, S30, Fit Nation and Profiles Gym.
Are there 24/7 gyms in Pāpāmoa? ▼
Yes. Snap Fitness at Fashion Island (3/34 Gravatt Road), Flex Fitness on Tara Road, Absolute Weights & Fitness at 29 Reynolds Place, and Anytime Fitness all offer 24/7 key-card access to members.
Where can I do CrossFit in Pāpāmoa? ▼
There are two CrossFit-affiliated facilities in Pāpāmoa. CrossFit Papamoa is at Unit 5, 9 Reynolds Road — no lock-in contracts, no joining fee, free 3-day trial. CrossFit Alliance is at 2/16 Ashley Place.
Does Pāpāmoa have a gym with Pilates and recovery? ▼
ReDefined Wellbeing Hub at 58 Ashley Place is New Zealand’s first fully integrated wellbeing centre. It offers reformer Pilates, yoga, hot yoga, strength training and open gym under one membership. The Recovery Lab includes two saunas, a cold plunge (12–4°C) and compression therapy. Allied health practitioners on site include physiotherapy (ACC-registered), clinical nutrition, DNA health testing and an integrative medicine GP.
What is Bout Hybrid? ▼
Bout Hybrid at 6/16 Ashley Place is Pāpāmoa’s dedicated hybrid training facility and official Hyrox club. Hybrid training combines strength and functional cardio in structured programmes. A free 7-day trial is available.
How much does a gym membership cost in Pāpāmoa? ▼
Pricing varies significantly by facility. Most 24/7 access gyms in Pāpāmoa start from around $20–$35 per week. Snap Fitness and Flex Fitness offer competitive rates with national or global reciprocal access included. Boutique facilities like ReDefined Wellbeing Hub and Bout Hybrid are priced higher to reflect the range of services. Contact individual gyms directly for current membership pricing.
