❓ Common Questions
How do I find a registered plumber in Pāpāmoa?▼
All plumbers must be registered with the Plumbers, Gasfitters and Drainlayers Board (PGDB). You can verify registration at pgdb.co.nz. Tap Masters is a confirmed Silver listing in Pāpāmoa. Other plumbers serving the area include Flow Right Plumbing, Papamoa Plumbing Co, Bay of Plenty Plumbing, Heat Pump & Hot Water, Golden Sands Plumbing and Coastal Drains. Always confirm PGDB registration before work begins.
Who do I call for a plumbing emergency in Pāpāmoa?▼
For a plumbing emergency such as a burst pipe or major leak, contact a local plumber directly. Tap Masters serves Pāpāmoa. If there is a water mains issue affecting the street, contact Tauranga City Council’s 24-hour fault line on 07 577 7000. For a gas leak, call your gas supplier’s emergency line and evacuate the building immediately.
Can a Pāpāmoa plumber do gas fitting?▼
Gas fitting requires a separate certification from the PGDB. Not all plumbers hold a gasfitting certificate. When enquiring about gas work, specifically ask whether the plumber holds a current gasfitter registration. Heat Pump & Hot Water and Bay of Plenty Plumbing both list gas fitting as a service.
What does a plumber charge per hour in the Bay of Plenty?▼
Plumber hourly rates in the Bay of Plenty typically range from $100 to $150 per hour plus GST. Call-out fees often apply for after-hours work. Most plumbers provide free quotes for larger jobs. Always confirm pricing before work begins.
