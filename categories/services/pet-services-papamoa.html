Keeping pets safe at Pāpāmoa Beach

Dogs are welcome on the beach at Pāpāmoa but must be on a leash in designated areas and under control at all times. Tauranga City Council’s bylaws designate certain beach zones as off-leash. Check the Tauranga City Council website for current beach zone maps. Salt water and sand can cause skin irritation in dogs - rinse your dog after beach visits.

Common Questions
Is there a vet in Pāpāmoa?▼
Pāpāmoa has a local vet clinic and is also served by Bay Veterinary Group which has multiple Bay of Plenty locations. For after-hours veterinary emergencies, contact the Bay Veterinary Group emergency line or the Tauranga 24-hour emergency vet service.
Are dogs allowed on Pāpāmoa Beach?▼
Dogs are allowed on the beach at Pāpāmoa, subject to Tauranga City Council bylaws. Dogs must be on a leash in designated leash areas and under control at all times. Some zones are off-leash at certain times - check current rules at tauranga.govt.nz.
Where can I board my dog near Pāpāmoa?▼
Golden Sands Kennels and Coastal Pet Sitting offer dog boarding options near Pāpāmoa. In-home pet sitting is also available for dogs who do better in a home environment. Contact individual businesses directly for current availability and pricing.
Where can I get my dog groomed in Pāpāmoa?▼
Doggy Do’s Grooming in Pāpāmoa offers full grooms, puppy trims, deshedding and bath and blow-dry services. ReDefined Wellbeing Hub’s dog grooming service is also listed. Book in advance as groomers in Pāpāmoa are often booked weeks ahead during summer.
