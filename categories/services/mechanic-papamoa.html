Coastal driving - salt air and your vehicle

Regular underbody washing and annual inspections are recommended for vehicles parked near the beach. Ask your mechanic to check brake callipers and underbody components at each service.

Common Questions
Where can I get a WOF in Pāpāmoa?▼
Coastal Autos at Ashley Place is Pāpāmoa’s Gold-listed WOF and servicing provider. Pāpāmoa also has Bay WOF Centre and several other workshops that perform WOF inspections. Most can accommodate same-day or next-day appointments for standard vehicles.
What is an MTA Approved repairer?▼
MTA Approved repairers have been independently assessed by the Motor Trade Association and meet standards for facilities, equipment, training and business practices. Using an MTA Approved workshop gives you assurance that the business operates to industry standards. Coastal Autos holds MTA Approved status.
Does salt air affect my car in Pāpāmoa?▼
Yes. Salt air in coastal areas like Pāpāmoa accelerates corrosion on brake components, exhaust systems, underbody metalwork and electrical connections. Regular underbody washing (monthly if parked near the beach), annual underbody inspections and quick attention to any rust are the best preventive measures.
Where can I get motorcycle servicing in Pāpāmoa?▼
Pāpāmoa Motorcycles at Ashley Place provides motorcycle servicing, parts and accessories. Contact them directly via papamoamotorcycles.co.nz for current service availability and booking.
