Tara Road Dental

📍 1G Tara Road, Pāpāmoa · New patients welcome

Modern, purpose-built dental practice at 1G Tara Road in Pāpāmoa, co-founded by Dr Lloyd Farrar. Offers a full range of general and cosmetic dentistry including implants, veneers, crowns and bridges. ❓ Common Questions
Which dentists in Pāpāmoa are accepting new patients?▼
Tara Road Dental at 1G Tara Road is currently accepting new patients. Phone 07 579 5291 to book. Pāpāmoa Orthodontist at the same address also accepts new patients for orthodontic consultations.
Does Pāpāmoa have a dentist that does IV sedation?▼
Yes. Tara Road Dental at 1G Tara Road offers IV sedation for anxious patients. This is arranged in advance — call 07 579 5291 to discuss your options before booking.
Where can I get Invisalign in Pāpāmoa?▼
Pāpāmoa Orthodontist at 1G Tara Road is an Invisalign provider. They also offer metal and ceramic braces and early treatment for children. Phone 07 579 3571.
Is there an emergency dentist in Pāpāmoa?▼
For dental emergencies during business hours, call Tara Road Dental on 07 579 5291. After hours, Tauranga has several after-hours dental services — search 'emergency dentist Tauranga' for current options. For severe facial swelling, go to Tauranga Hospital Emergency or call 111.
