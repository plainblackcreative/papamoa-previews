🚨 Urgent & After-Hours Care

🚨 Life-threatening emergency? Call 111.

For non-emergency urgent care in Pāpāmoa: Pāpāmoa Urgent Care, Shop 4, 2 Gravatt Road — open 7 days, 8am–8pm. Phone: 07 574 9999. After 8pm, the nearest after-hours option is the Tauranga After Hours Medical Centre at 89 Cameron Road, Tauranga. Tauranga Hospital Emergency Department is approximately 15 minutes drive from central Pāpāmoa.

❓ Common Questions
What medical centres are in Pāpāmoa?▼
Pāpāmoa has two main GP practices: Pāpāmoa East Medical Centre (07 542 2020) and Tara Road Medical (07 574 8780). Both operate Monday to Friday and take enrolled patients. After-hours urgent care is available at Pāpāmoa Urgent Care (07 574 9999, 8am–8pm daily) and the after-hours clinic at Tauranga Hospital.
Where are the dentists in Pāpāmoa?▼
Tara Road Dental at 1G Tara Road (07 579 5291) offers general and cosmetic dentistry, implants, veneers, crowns and IV sedation. They accept new patients. Pāpāmoa Orthodontist at the same address provides braces, Invisalign and early orthodontic treatment.
Where is the pharmacy in Pāpāmoa?▼
Pāpāmoa has two pharmacies: Unichem Pāpāmoa at Pāpāmoa Plaza (07 572 3790) and Life Pharmacy at The Sands Town Centre (07 572 1870). Both provide prescriptions, vaccinations and healthcare advice.
Is there a physiotherapist in Pāpāmoa?▼
Yes. ReDefined Wellbeing Hub at 58 Ashley Place has an ACC-registered physiotherapy practice on site (07 542 0576). The Physio Collective and Pāpāmoa Physio are also listed. Most accept direct ACC referrals, meaning most clients face minimal upfront cost.
Where is urgent care in Pāpāmoa?▼
Pāpāmoa Urgent Care at Shop 4, 2 Gravatt Road is open 7 days, 8am–8pm (07 574 9999). For after-hours beyond 8pm, the Tauranga After Hours Medical Centre at 89 Cameron Road is the nearest option. Dial 111 for life-threatening emergencies.
Is there an optometrist or eye specialist in Pāpāmoa?▼
Yes. Tauranga Eye Specialists opened a Pāpāmoa clinic at 21/39 Toorea Street in June 2024 (07 578 7508). They offer specialist ophthalmology and full eye care services. Pāpāmoa Optometrists on Domain Road also provides optometry services including eye tests and glasses.
Can I get IV sedation at a Pāpāmoa dentist?▼
Yes. Tara Road Dental at 1G Tara Road offers IV sedation for anxious patients. This is arranged in advance — contact the practice on 07 579 5291 to discuss your options.
