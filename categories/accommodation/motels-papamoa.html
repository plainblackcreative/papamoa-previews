Frequently Asked Questions

What motels are in Pāpāmoa?

Pāpāmoa has several motels including Beach House Motel (3-minute walk to beach, pool and spa, owners Gina and Pete), Bay Palm Motel (within 150 metres of the beach, outdoor pool), and Energy Motor Lodge Pāpāmoa. All offer self-contained units with kitchenette facilities.

How far are Pāpāmoa motels from the beach?

Beach House Motel is approximately a 3-minute walk to the beach. Bay Palm Motel is within 150 metres. Most Pāpāmoa motels are within easy walking distance of the 16km coastline.

Do Pāpāmoa motels book out for AIMS Games?

Yes. Pāpāmoa accommodation fills quickly during the Zespri AIMS Games each September. Book 3–6 months ahead for AIMS Games week. Pacific Park and Beyonder holiday homes are also popular AIMS Games options.

What does a Pāpāmoa motel cost per night?

Motel rates in Pāpāmoa typically range from around $160 to $280 per night for two people, depending on the property and season. Peak summer and AIMS Games week attract higher rates. Always book direct or check booking sites for current pricing.

Are Pāpāmoa motels pet-friendly?

Most Pāpāmoa motels do not accept pets. For pet-friendly accommodation, private holiday homes on Airbnb and Bookabach are the best option — filter for pet-friendly when searching.
