Holiday Parks & Camping

Holiday Parks in Pāpāmoa

Pacific Park at 1110 Pāpāmoa Beach Road is alcohol and smoke-free with campsites, cabins, motel units and group lodges. Tasman Holiday Parks is the only beachfront holiday park, sitting directly on the sand with villas, cabins and powered sites. See the Holiday Parks page.

Motels in Pāpāmoa
