AIMS Games 2026 — book accommodation now

The Zespri AIMS Games run 5–11 September 2026, bringing 12,000+ students and families to Tauranga. Pāpāmoa accommodation books out completely during AIMS Games week every year. If you are planning to attend, secure accommodation well in advance. Full AIMS Games guide →
