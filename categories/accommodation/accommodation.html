⭐ Gold Listing Pāpāmoa’s top-rated accommodation

Pacific Park Christian Holiday Camp

🏠 Holiday Park · 1110 Pāpāmoa Beach Road

Pacific Park is consistently Pāpāmoa’s highest-rated accommodation on TripAdvisor — a well-maintained, alcohol and smoke-free holiday park set on five acres just a two-minute walk from the beach. It offers the full range from powered campsites through to fully serviced motel units, villas, cabins and self-contained group lodges — all within a family-friendly, secure environment. Quiet hours from 10pm, no parties, no pets. Pacific Park regularly hosts school groups, sports teams, church camps and family reunions alongside regular holiday guests.
