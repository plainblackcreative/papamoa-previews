📍 Pāpāmoa Beach Road · Since 1944 · Alcohol & smoke-free
★★★★★ 4.8 — TripAdvisor #1 Pāpāmoa (321 reviews)

Pacific Park Christian Holiday Camp has operated near Pāpāmoa Beach since 1944 and is consistently rated the number one accommodation in Pāpāmoa on TripAdvisor. ❓ Common Questions
What holiday parks are in Pāpāmoa?▼
Pāpāmoa has two main holiday parks: Pacific Park Christian Holiday Camp (TripAdvisor #1 rated, alcohol and smoke-free, on Pāpāmoa Beach Road) and Pāpāmoa Beach Resort at 535 Pāpāmoa Beach Road. Both offer powered and non-powered sites, cabins and motel-style units. Tasman Holiday Parks also operates a beachfront site in Pāpāmoa.
Is Pacific Park Pāpāmoa alcohol-free?▼
Yes. Pacific Park Christian Holiday Camp is smoke, vape and alcohol-free throughout the property. It has operated near Pāpāmoa Beach since 1944 and is consistently rated the top accommodation in Pāpāmoa on TripAdvisor with over 321 reviews.
Which Pāpāmoa holiday parks are suitable for school camps?▼
Pacific Park has purpose-built group lodge facilities and a long history of hosting school camps, sports teams and group stays. The alcohol and smoke-free policy makes it particularly well-suited for school groups. Contact them directly to discuss group bookings and availability.
Do Pāpāmoa holiday parks book out for AIMS Games?▼
Yes. Accommodation across Pāpāmoa books out completely for the Zespri AIMS Games held each September. Pacific Park is a popular AIMS Games base for school groups and sports teams. Book as early as possible — ideally as soon as AIMS Games registrations open. See the full AIMS Games guide.
Does Pāpāmoa have beachfront camping?▼
Yes. Pāpāmoa Beach Resort at 535 Pāpāmoa Beach Road sits directly on Pāpāmoa Beach Road with beach access. Pacific Park is a two-minute walk from the beach. Tasman Holiday Parks also sits directly on the sand. See the Camping & Campsites page for powered site details.
