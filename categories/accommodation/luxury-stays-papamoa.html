❓ Common Questions
Is there luxury accommodation in Pāpāmoa?▼
Pāpāmoa has a growing range of premium short-stay and boutique accommodation, particularly beachfront apartments and self-contained homes. The Reef Beachfront Apartments and Tasman Holiday Parks spa villas are among the more premium options currently available. The luxury market in Pāpāmoa is less developed than in Mount Maunganui, but beachfront properties are in high demand.
Where can I find beachfront accommodation in Pāpāmoa?▼
Pāpāmoa Beach stretches 16 kilometres along the Pacific Ocean. Beachfront options include The Reef Beachfront Apartments, Belle Mer Beachfront Apartments and Tasman Holiday Parks luxury and spa villas. Premium holiday homes on Airbnb and Bookabach also offer beachfront access. See the Apartments page for more detail.
Are there spa villas or spa accommodation in Pāpāmoa?▼
Tasman Holiday Parks offers spa villas directly on Pāpāmoa Beach — self-contained premium units with private spa pools. These are among the most in-demand accommodation in Pāpāmoa and book out months in advance for summer and AIMS Games week in September.
Is Pāpāmoa better or worse than Mount Maunganui for luxury accommodation?▼
Mount Maunganui has a more developed luxury accommodation market, with boutique hotels and upscale apartments. Pāpāmoa offers a quieter, more residential beachside feel with fewer but more exclusive premium options — particularly beachfront homes and spa villas. Pāpāmoa is a 10-minute drive from the Mount, making either a practical base for exploring both areas.
What is the best premium accommodation in Pāpāmoa?▼
The Reef Beachfront Apartments is consistently the most-reviewed premium apartment in Pāpāmoa, praised for spacious interiors and oversized ocean-view balconies. Tasman Holiday Parks spa villas offer a beachfront spa pool experience. High-end holiday homes on Airbnb and Bookabach are the most flexible option for larger groups wanting a genuinely premium whole-home stay.
