Common Questions
What self-contained accommodation is in Pāpāmoa?▼
Pāpāmoa has several self-contained options including Pacific Palms Motel (kitchenette units, short walk to beach), The Reef Beachfront Apartments and Belle Mer Beachfront Apartments (both beachfront with full kitchen), Bay Palm Motel (within 150m of beach, kitchenette) and various holiday homes via Airbnb and Bookabach.
What does self-contained mean for accommodation in New Zealand?▼
Self-contained accommodation in New Zealand means the unit has its own kitchen or kitchenette, bathroom and private entrance. You do not share facilities with other guests or the host. For motorhome and freedom camping purposes, self-contained also has a specific legal definition that includes a toilet and fresh water supply.
Is self-contained accommodation good for families in Pāpāmoa?▼
Yes. Self-contained units allow cooking your own meals, flexible mealtimes and more space than a standard hotel room. Most self-contained properties in Pāpāmoa are within easy reach of supermarkets at Pāpāmoa Plaza and The Sands, and close to the beach.
Is self-contained accommodation in Pāpāmoa pet-friendly?▼
Most self-contained motel and apartment units in Pāpāmoa do not accept pets. Private holiday homes via Airbnb and Bookabach offer the best pet-friendly options - use the pet-friendly filter when searching. See the Pet-Friendly Accommodation page for more detail.
What is the difference between self-contained and serviced accommodation in Pāpāmoa?▼
Self-contained accommodation has its own kitchen, bathroom and private entrance - you prepare your own food and have full independence. Serviced accommodation like B&Bs includes meals and a more hosted experience. Motels in Pāpāmoa are typically self-contained with kitchenettes. Beachfront apartments are fully self-contained with full kitchens. See the B&Bs page for hosted options.
