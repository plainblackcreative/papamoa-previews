Common Questions
Are there beachfront apartments in Pāpāmoa?▼
Yes. Pāpāmoa has several beachfront and beach-adjacent apartment options including The Reef Beachfront Apartments and Belle Mer Beachfront Apartments, both on Pāpāmoa Beach Road. Both offer self-contained units with ocean views and direct beach access.
What is the best apartment accommodation in Pāpāmoa?▼
The Reef Beachfront Apartments consistently receives the strongest reviews, particularly for spacious interiors, modern fit-out and oversized ocean-view balconies. Guests regularly cite the balcony as a highlight of the stay. Belle Mer Beachfront Apartments is also well-regarded for its direct beach access.
Do Pāpāmoa apartments have full kitchens?▼
Most holiday apartments in Pāpāmoa are fully self-contained with kitchen facilities, making them practical for families and longer stays. Always confirm kitchen facilities when booking, as some properties offer kitchenettes rather than full kitchens.
How far in advance should I book a Pāpāmoa apartment?▼
Book 3 to 6 months ahead for summer (December to February) and the Zespri AIMS Games in September. Beachfront apartments fill well before peak periods. For AIMS Games 2026 (5–11 September), book as early as possible. See the full AIMS Games guide.
How far are Pāpāmoa apartments from the beach?▼
Beachfront apartments in Pāpāmoa are directly on or adjacent to Pāpāmoa Beach Road, within a short walk of the water. The Reef Beachfront Apartments and Belle Mer Beachfront Apartments both have direct beach access.
