Frequently Asked Questions

Who manages rental properties in Pāpāmoa?

The main agencies are Eves Rentals (Pāpāmoa Plaza, 07 572 8285) and Ray White Pāpāmoa (rwpapamoa.co.nz). Both manage residential properties across Pāpāmoa, Pāpāmoa East and Golden Sands.

What is the average rent in Pāpāmoa?

As of 2025–2026, median weekly rents range from approximately $500–$600 per week for a 3-bedroom home. The market is competitive. Check current listings on TradeMe Property for accurate pricing.

Is Pāpāmoa good for renters?

Pāpāmoa is popular with renters for its beach access, good schools, family-friendly community and new housing stock in Pāpāmoa East and Golden Sands. Tauranga CBD is about 15 minutes away.

How do I find a rental in Pāpāmoa?

Check TradeMe Property, evesrentals.co.nz and rwpapamoa.co.nz for current listings. Facebook groups for Pāpāmoa and Tauranga rentals are also active with private landlord listings.
