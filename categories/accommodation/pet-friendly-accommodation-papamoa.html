🐶 Dog Rules at Pāpāmoa Beach

What you need to know before bringing your dog

  • On-lead period: Dogs must be on-lead between 9am and 5pm from 1 October to 30 April on most Pāpāmoa beach sections.
  • Off-leash times: Outside the on-lead period (before 9am and after 5pm in summer, and throughout winter), dogs may be off-lead on most sections.
  • Designated areas: Some reserve and park areas near Pāpāmoa have designated off-leash dog exercise areas. Check with Tauranga City Council for current locations.
  • Always clean up: Dog waste bags and bins are required by council bylaw. Always clean up after your dog on the beach and in public areas.
  • Current rules: Rules can change seasonally. Always confirm current bylaws at tauranga.govt.nz before your visit.

Pāpāmoa’s long beach is one of the best in the Bay of Plenty for dogs. The wide, uncrowded sections away from the main access points are ideal for off-leash exercise in the early morning and evening during summer.

❓ Common Questions
Is there pet-friendly accommodation in Pāpāmoa?▼
Yes, but it is primarily private holiday homes rather than motels or holiday parks. The best approach is to search Airbnb or Bookabach with the pet-friendly filter for Pāpāmoa properties. Several private homes in Pāpāmoa East and Golden Sands explicitly allow dogs.
Can I bring my dog to Pāpāmoa Beach?▼
Yes, with restrictions. Dogs must be on-lead between 9am and 5pm from 1 October to 30 April on most Pāpāmoa beach sections. Before 9am and after 5pm during summer, and throughout winter, dogs may be off-lead. Always check the Tauranga City Council website for current bylaws before your visit as regulations can change.
Do the holiday parks in Pāpāmoa allow pets?▼
Pacific Park Christian Holiday Camp does not allow pets. For Pāpāmoa Beach Resort, confirm directly as pet policies can vary by season. Most holiday parks in Pāpāmoa do not accept animals. Private holiday homes are the best option for dog owners.
Which booking platforms have pet-friendly filters for Pāpāmoa?▼
Airbnb and Bookabach both have pet-friendly filters that work well for Pāpāmoa. Search for Pāpāmoa and filter by “pets allowed” to see current availability. Always confirm the pet policy directly with the host before booking.
Are there off-leash dog areas near Pāpāmoa?▼
Yes. Tauranga City Council designates some beach sections as off-leash at certain times and seasons. Pāpāmoa Domain and some reserve areas also permit off-leash exercise. Check the Tauranga City Council website for current designated dog exercise areas near Pāpāmoa.
