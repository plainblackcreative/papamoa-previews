Fishing Gear Tackle, rods & supplies
🎣Nearest
Hunting & Fishing Tauranga
📍 Cameron Road, Tauranga · approx. 20 min from Pāpāmoa

The Bay of Plenty’s best-stocked fishing and hunting retailer. Surf casting, estuary, offshore and game fishing gear. Rods, reels, braid, leader, softbaits, lures, hooks, sinkers, berley and bait. Staff fish the region themselves and can advise on local conditions, species and rigs for snapper, kahawai, kingfish and trevally. Also stocks camping, hunting clothing and firearms (licence required).

Outdoors & adventure near Pāpāmoa — common questions

Pāpāmoa does not have a dedicated fishing tackle shop. The nearest specialist is Hunting and Fishing Tauranga on Cameron Road (about 20 minutes). Mitre 10 on Gravatt Road and The Warehouse at Pāpāmoa Plaza stock basic rods, reels and tackle for casual surf casting.
Torpedo7 at Bayfair Mall in Mount Maunganui (about 15 minutes) and Hunting and Fishing Tauranga on Cameron Road (about 20 minutes) are the closest dedicated camping retailers. Mitre 10 in Pāpāmoa stocks gas canisters, tarps and camping basics.
Hunting and Fishing Tauranga on Cameron Road is the nearest full-service hunting retailer, about 20 minutes from Pāpāmoa. They stock firearms, ammunition, hunting clothing, optics and knives. A firearms licence is required for firearm purchases in New Zealand.
Basic surf casting rods and combos are available at The Warehouse and Mitre 10 in Pāpāmoa. For a wider range including specialist spinning, jigging and estuary rods, Hunting and Fishing Tauranga on Cameron Road has the best local selection.
Pāpāmoa Beach is a productive surf casting location. Common species include snapper, kahawai, trevally, gurnard and the occasional kingfish. Kahawai run the beach regularly in summer and autumn. No fishing licence is required for recreational saltwater fishing in New Zealand.
