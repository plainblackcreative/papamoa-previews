Computers & phones in Pāpāmoa — common questions

There is a mobile phone accessories kiosk in Pāpāmoa stocking phone cases, screen protectors, chargers and cables. The Warehouse at Pāpāmoa Plaza also carries mobile accessories. For a wider selection, JB Hi-Fi at Bayfair Mall is about 15 minutes away.
There is a PC sales and repair store on Domain Road in Pāpāmoa, next to KFC. They sell PC components and offer repair services. For a wider range of laptops, JB Hi-Fi at Bayfair Mall and Noel Leeming in Tauranga are approximately 15 to 20 minutes away.
The Warehouse at Pāpāmoa Plaza stocks a basic range of laptops and Chromebooks. For a wider selection, JB Hi-Fi at Bayfair Mall and Noel Leeming and Harvey Norman in Tauranga carry most major brands. Both are approximately 15 to 20 minutes from Pāpāmoa.
Pāpāmoa does not have standalone telco stores. The nearest are in Tauranga CBD or at Bayfair Mall, about 15 to 20 minutes away. The Warehouse at Pāpāmoa Plaza sells prepay SIMs and some handsets from multiple networks.
