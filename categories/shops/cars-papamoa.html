Car Hire & Dealerships
near Pāpāmoa

There are no car dealerships or rental depots in Pāpāmoa itself — but Tauranga Airport is 15 minutes away with all the major rental companies, and Hewletts Road in Mount Maunganui has the Bay of Plenty’s largest strip of car dealerships, about 20 minutes from Pāpāmoa.

✈️
Arriving by air? Pick up your rental at Tauranga Airport (TRG) and you’ll be at Pāpāmoa Beach in about 15 minutes via SH2. All major rental companies have counters inside the terminal.

Car Rental — Tauranga Airport 15 min from Pāpāmoa · 73 Jean Batten Drive
📍
All at Tauranga Airport, 73 Jean Batten Drive, Mount Maunganui Rental counters are inside the terminal at the arrivals area. From the airport, Pāpāmoa is a straight 15-minute drive east on SH2. Cars & vehicle hire near Pāpāmoa — common questions

Tauranga Airport (TRG) is the nearest rental hub, about 15 minutes from Pāpāmoa via SH2. All major companies operate from 73 Jean Batten Drive: Apex, Avis, Budget, Ezi, Hertz and Thrifty, all with counters inside the terminal. Rite Price Rentals also has a depot near the airport for budget options.
Tauranga Airport is approximately 15 minutes from central Pāpāmoa via State Highway 2. It is at 73 Jean Batten Drive, Mount Maunganui. The airport serves domestic routes to Auckland, Wellington and Christchurch.
There are no car dealerships in Pāpāmoa itself. The nearest are on Hewletts Road in Mount Maunganui, about 20 minutes away. Farmer Autovillage (12 brands including Nissan, Audi, VW, Subaru), TMC (Kia, Suzuki, Honda), Ebbett Prestige (Jaguar, Land Rover, Volvo) and Ultimate Motor Group (Ford, Mazda) are all within a 2km strip.
The quickest route is via State Highway 2 east through Mount Maunganui, taking about 15 minutes. A rental car is the most convenient option. Taxis and rideshare services also operate from the airport. There is no direct bus service between the airport and Pāpāmoa.
Rates vary by company, season and vehicle type. As a guide, compact cars (such as a Toyota Yaris or Suzuki Swift) start around $45–$63 per day. SUVs run $60–$100+ per day. Booking at least a week in advance generally gets a better rate. Ezi Car Rental is among the highest-rated providers at TRG.

🚗 At a Glance

  • Tauranga Airport (TRG) — 15 min from Pāpāmoa
  • 73 Jean Batten Drive, Mt Maunganui
  • Rental brands: Apex, Avis, Budget, Ezi, Hertz, Thrifty
  • All counters inside the terminal
  • Avg compact car: ~$45–$63/day

  • Farmer Autovillage — 12 brands at 116 Hewletts Rd
