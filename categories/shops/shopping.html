Xanadu Book Exchange

📚 Second-hand Books · 2C Ashley Place, Pāpāmoa East

A warehouse-scale second-hand bookstore — the only one left in the Bay of Plenty. Around 10 million books in stock at any time, all colour-coded by price with no book over $5. Also stocks DVDs, CDs, vinyl records and jigsaws. Owner Briar Simons pays cash or store credit for quality used books.
