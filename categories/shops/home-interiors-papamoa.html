The Warehouse Pāpāmoa
Pāpāmoa Plaza, Gravatt Road

The Warehouse at Pāpāmoa Plaza is the suburb’s main general merchandise retailer and covers a wide range of home and living needs. Furniture includes flat-pack storage, shelving, beds, tables and chairs. Homewares cover kitchen, bathroom and bedroom basics. The linen, bedding and cushion range is reliable for everyday needs without specialist pricing. Also stocks a basic range of small appliances and cooking equipment. Open 7 days.

Berridge Appliance
Domain Road, Pāpāmoa

Berridge Appliance is a local independent appliance retailer on Domain Road. They stock whiteware and household appliances including fridges, washing machines, dryers and dishwashers. An independent local option worth checking before heading to a big-box retailer in Tauranga. Confirm current range and availability by contacting them directly.

Home & interiors in Pāpāmoa - common questions

The Warehouse at Pāpāmoa Plaza stocks flat-pack and ready-to-assemble furniture including shelving, storage, beds, tables and chairs. For a wider range, Smiths City, Nood and Forty Winks are in central Tauranga or at Bayfair Mall in Mount Maunganui, both about 15 to 20 minutes away.
Berridge Appliance on Domain Road is a local independent appliance retailer in Pāpāmoa. The Warehouse at Pāpāmoa Plaza also carries a basic range. For a wider selection, Harvey Norman at Bayfair Mall and Noel Leeming in Tauranga are the main appliance retailers in the area, about 15 to 20 minutes away.
There is no dedicated bed retailer within Pāpāmoa. The Warehouse stocks basic bed frames and bedding. For specialist mattresses and beds, Forty Winks and Bedshed operate at Bayfair Mall, approximately 15 minutes from Pāpāmoa.
The Warehouse at Pāpāmoa Plaza carries homewares, kitchen items, linen, cushions, rugs and basic decor. For a broader range, Kmart and Briscoes at Bayfair Mall offer more selection. Boutique gift and homewares shops at Fashion Island and The Sands sometimes stock decorative items.
There is no curtain or blinds specialist within Pāpāmoa. Spotlight in Tauranga stocks fabric and ready-made curtains and is about 20 minutes away. The Warehouse has a basic range of ready-made curtains in-suburb.
