Plum Organics Pāpāmoa
📍 Suite 2, 22 Gravatt Road, Pāpāmoa Beach  ·  📞 07 574 2160  ·  plumorganics.co.nz
🕐 Mon–Fri 9am–5:30pm · Sat 9am–5pm · Sun 10am–4pm

Plum Organics is Pāpāmoa’s community health and organic store, next to Fashion Island on Gravatt Road. A local institution with 2,800+ Facebook followers and a part of the Community Health Stores network. They stock a wide range of organic fruit and vegetables, natural vitamin and mineral supplements, protein powder, collagen, skincare and beauty, whole foods and health treatments. The team are experienced and qualified — happy to discuss ingredients and assist with specific health needs. A genuine alternative to chain pharmacy supplements with a focus on quality, whole-food and naturopathic brands.
