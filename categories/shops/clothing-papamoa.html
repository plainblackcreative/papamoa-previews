Where can I buy clothes in Pāpāmoa? ⌄
Fashion Island at 42 Gravatt Road is Pāpāmoa's main clothing destination with 18+ stores including Puma, Cotton On, Under Armour, Skechers, Bonds, Bendon, Converse and Decjuba. Pāpāmoa Plaza also has clothing retailers. Bayfair Mall is 15 minutes away.
What brands are at Fashion Island Papamoa? ⌄
Fashion Island hosts Puma, Cotton On, Under Armour, Skechers, Bonds, Bendon, Converse, Decjuba and others. It opened in 2006 and is open 7 days 9am–5pm.
What time does Fashion Island open? ⌄
Fashion Island is open 7 days a week from 9am to 5pm.
