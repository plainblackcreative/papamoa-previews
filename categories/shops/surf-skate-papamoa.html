❓ Common questions
Is there a surf shop in Pāpāmoa? ⌄
Yes. Pāpāmoa has two: Backdoor at Pāpāmoa Plaza (NZ's largest surf/skate/streetwear store) and Switch Stance at Unit 10, 2 Ashley Place (locally owned, specialising in surfskates — largest range in NZ).
What is Switch Stance in Papamoa? ⌄
Switch Stance Surf & Skate at Unit 10, 2 Ashley Place is locally owned by Shelly Jones, a 30+ year surfer. It specialises in surfskates and has the largest surfskate range in New Zealand. Known for being women-friendly and offering genuine advice.
Does Backdoor Papamoa stock surfboards? ⌄
Yes. Backdoor carries hundreds of new surfboards from JS Industries, Channel Islands, Firewire and Rusty, plus wetsuits and apparel from Billabong, Rip Curl, Volcom, Hurley and more.
