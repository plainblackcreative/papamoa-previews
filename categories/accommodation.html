⭐ Gold Spotlight Pāpāmoa’s top-rated accommodation

Pacific Park Christian Holiday Camp

🏠 Holiday Park · 1110 Pāpāmoa Beach Road

Pacific Park is consistently Pāpāmoa’s highest-rated accommodation on TripAdvisor — a well-maintained, alcohol and smoke-free holiday park set on five acres just a two-minute walk from the beach. It offers the full range from powered campsites through to fully serviced motel units, villas, cabins and self-contained group lodges — all within a family-friendly, secure environment. Quiet hours from 10pm, no parties, no pets. The atmosphere is relaxed and well-run. Pacific Park regularly hosts school groups, sports teams, church camps and family reunions alongside regular holiday guests.

Tasman Holiday Parks — Pāpāmoa Beach
📍 Pāpāmoa Beach Road — Absolute beachfront

The only truly beachfront holiday park in Pāpāmoa, Tasman Holiday Parks sits directly on the sand with a range of accommodation from luxury beachfront villas and spa villas through to basic cabins and powered sites. Resort-style facilities include a heated pool, hot pools, playground and BBQ area. Well-regarded by families and summer regulars. Books out well in advance for AIMS Games and summer holidays.
