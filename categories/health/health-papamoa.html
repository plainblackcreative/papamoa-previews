Pāpāmoa Orthodontist

🫀 Braces · Clear Aligners · Early Treatment · 1G Tara Road, Pāpāmoa

Specialised orthodontic care for children, teens and adults at 1G Tara Road, Pāpāmoa Beach. Metal braces, ceramic braces, clear aligners (Invisalign) and early treatment are all available. ❓ Common Questions
What health services are available in Pāpāmoa? ▼
Pāpāmoa has a broad range of health services including GP medical centres, dentists, an orthodontist, physiotherapy, pharmacy, optometry and integrated wellbeing. Key providers include Pāpāmoa Medical Centre, Pāpāmoa Beach Medical Centre, Tara Road Dental, Pāpāmoa Orthodontist, ReDefined Wellbeing Hub (physiotherapy, clinical nutrition, integrative medicine GP), Unichem Papamoa, Life Pharmacy and Specsavers at Papamoa Plaza.
Is there a doctor or GP in Pāpāmoa? ▼
Yes. Pāpāmoa has multiple medical centres including Pāpāmoa Medical Centre and Pāpāmoa Beach Medical Centre. Both offer enrolled and casual GP appointments. For urgent care outside of normal hours, Tauranga Accident & Urgent Medical Care on Cameron Road is the nearest option. For life-threatening emergencies call 111.
Where is the nearest dentist in Pāpāmoa? ▼
Tara Road Dental at 1G Tara Road, Pāpāmoa is a modern purpose-built dental practice. Services include general dentistry, hygienist, implants, veneers, crowns, wisdom tooth removal and IV sedation for anxious patients. ACC dental injury appointments are available daily. Call 07 579 5291.
Does Pāpāmoa have an orthodontist? ▼
Yes. Pāpāmoa Orthodontist at 1G Tara Road offers specialised orthodontic care for children, teens and adults. Metal braces, ceramic braces, Invisalign clear aligners and early treatment are all available. NZAO member. Comprehensive fees cover all treatment including retainers. Free parking on site. Call 07 579 3571.
Where can I get physiotherapy in Pāpāmoa? ▼
ReDefined Wellbeing Hub at 58 Ashley Place has ACC-registered physiotherapists on site as part of their integrated health model. Several independent physiotherapy providers also operate in the wider Pāpāmoa area. Phone your preferred provider to confirm ACC funding and appointment availability.
Is there urgent care or after-hours medical help near Pāpāmoa? ▼
Pāpāmoa does not have a dedicated urgent care facility. For urgent needs outside GP hours, head to Tauranga Accident & Urgent Medical Care on Cameron Road (extended hours, no appointment needed). For serious emergencies go to Tauranga Hospital Emergency Department or call 111. You can also call Healthline free on 0800 611 116 for advice 24 hours a day.
