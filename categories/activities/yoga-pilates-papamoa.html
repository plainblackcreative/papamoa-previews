Mahana Yoga & Pilates

🤼 Yoga, Pilates & Barre · 552 Pāpāmoa Beach Road

A boutique yoga and pilates studio on Pāpāmoa Beach Road, founded by Freya White whose background spans nursing and teacher training across India, Costa Rica, Bali and New Zealand. Mahana offers yoga, pilates, barre and yin classes across a range of styles from high-intensity pilates burn sessions to slow, restorative flow. Known locally for its warm atmosphere and the sense of calm from the moment you arrive. Online booking required.

❓ Common Questions
Is there a yoga studio in Pāpāmoa?+
Yes. Mahana Yoga & Pilates at 552 Pāpāmoa Beach Road is a boutique studio offering yoga, pilates, barre and yin. ReDefined Wellbeing Hub at Ashley Place has a dedicated yoga studio. Anytime Fitness The Sands and Fit Nation Pāpāmoa also run yoga classes.
Is there reformer pilates in Pāpāmoa?+
Yes — Pāpāmoa has several reformer options. KX Pilates offers high-intensity 50-minute sessions. BURN Reformer Pilates runs small-group sessions (max 6 reformers) from CrossFit Alliance. Reform Fitness has its own Pāpāmoa studio. ReDefined Wellbeing Hub and Fit Nation also have reformer equipment.
Where is Mahana Yoga & Pilates?+
Mahana Yoga & Pilates is at 552 Pāpāmoa Beach Road. Founded by Freya White, the studio runs yoga, pilates, barre and yin classes. Online booking is required via mahanayogaloft.co.nz.
What is KX Pilates?+
KX Pilates is a reformer pilates studio combining traditional pilates with high-intensity progressive techniques. All sessions are 50 minutes and designed for any fitness level. The Pāpāmoa location is one of several in the Bay of Plenty area.
Can I try a yoga or pilates class casually without a membership?+
Yes. Mahana Yoga & Pilates offers single class passes valid for one month from purchase. KX Pilates is bookable via ClassPass. Most studios offer a trial or casual option — check directly with each studio for current pricing.
