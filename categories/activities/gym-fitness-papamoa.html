ReDefined Wellbeing Hub

🏋 Integrated Fitness & Wellness · Ashley Place, Pāpāmoa

Pāpāmoa’s flagship fitness and wellness centre — five specialised studios under one roof covering reformer pilates, yoga, strength and conditioning, open gym and an advanced recovery lab. Led by founder Jimmy August, ReDefined describes its Pāpāmoa location as the original site and blueprint for their national network. New Zealand’s first integrated facility of this kind, with physiotherapy, nutrition and massage also available on site. The recovery lab includes infrared saunas, ice baths and compression therapy.

❓ Common Questions
Is there a gym in Pāpāmoa?+
Yes. Pāpāmoa has several options. ReDefined Wellbeing Hub at Ashley Place is an integrated fitness and wellness centre. Fit Nation opened in January 2026 with 24/7 access. Anytime Fitness operates at The Sands in Pāpāmoa East. Multiple pilates and yoga studios also serve the area.
Is there a 24-hour gym in Pāpāmoa?+
Yes. Fit Nation Pāpāmoa offers 24/7 access and opened in January 2026. Anytime Fitness at The Sands also provides 24-hour access for members.
Where can I do reformer pilates in Pāpāmoa?+
ReDefined Wellbeing Hub has a dedicated reformer pilates studio at Ashley Place. Fit Nation also offers reformer pilates. For specialist-only pilates studios, KX Pilates, BURN Reformer Pilates, Mahana and Reform Fitness are all in the area — see the Yoga & Pilates page.
Is there a gym with a sauna or recovery facilities in Pāpāmoa?+
Yes. ReDefined Wellbeing Hub has a dedicated Recovery Lab with infrared saunas, ice baths and compression therapy. Fit Nation Pāpāmoa also includes saunas and ice baths in their wellness offering.
Does Pāpāmoa have personal training?+
Yes. ReDefined Wellbeing Hub, Fit Nation and Anytime Fitness The Sands all offer personal training. ReDefined also has on-site physiotherapy, nutrition and massage as part of their integrated wellbeing approach. Several independent personal trainers also operate across the suburb.
