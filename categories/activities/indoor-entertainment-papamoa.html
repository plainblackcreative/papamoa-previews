The Cave Pāpāmoa Spotlight
7/16 Ashley Place, Pāpāmoa East 0800 228 369 Ages 8+ Groups up to 40

The Cave at Ashley Place is Pāpāmoa’s dedicated indoor entertainment destination. They offer hosted virtual reality sessions, VR escape rooms, sim racing and PC gaming for ages 8 and up. A state-of-the-art facility - whether you want to hire the whole venue or book a group activity, their team customises your experience from start to finish. Popular for birthday parties, stag and hens dos, and corporate team building events up to 40 people. Gift vouchers available. Book online or call.
