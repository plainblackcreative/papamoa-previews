⭐ Gold Listing Surf Schools · Pāpāmoa

Hakanini Surf School & Rentals

🏄 Surf School · Pāpāmoa Domain

Pāpāmoa’s dedicated surf school, operating from the Domain with group and private lessons for all ages and abilities. School holiday programmes run throughout summer. Board hire from $35 half-day. Strong emphasis on ocean safety and reading conditions — ideal for first-timers and those returning to the water.

🎮 Indoor & VR Entertainment
⭐ Gold Listing Indoor Entertainment · Pāpāmoa

The Cave Pāpāmoa

🎮 VR & Sim Racing · 7/16 Ashley Place, Pāpāmoa East

New Zealand’s home for virtual reality experiences, VR escape rooms and sim racing. One of three Cave locations nationwide, with hosted sessions for all ages and abilities. Birthday parties, school groups and corporate events catered for — the venue can host groups up to 40. Ages 8 and up for most experiences.

🏋 Gym & Fitness
🏋
ReDefined Wellbeing Hub
📍 Ashley Place, Pāpāmoa · Gym, Pilates & Physio

Pāpāmoa’s flagship integrated health and fitness centre — five specialised studios covering reformer pilates, yoga, strength and conditioning, open gym and a recovery lab with infrared saunas, ice baths and compression therapy. Physiotherapy, nutrition and massage also on site. New Zealand’s first facility of this kind.

Fishing at Pāpāmoa Beach

Pāpāmoa Beach is a popular surf-casting spot with snapper, kahawai and trevally year-round. Multiple beach access points along the 16km stretch. No licence required for recreational saltwater fishing. See the Fishing page for spots, tides and charters, or the community fishing guide for live bite times.
