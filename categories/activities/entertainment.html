Beach & Water Activities

Pāpāmoa's 16km surf beach is the hub — learn-to-surf and board hire at the Domain, SUP and kayaking on calm mornings, surfcasting for snapper and kahawai (no licence needed), and patrolled swimming between the flags October to April.

Indoor & VR Entertainment

Wet-day and evening fun — virtual reality, escape rooms and sim racing for ages 8+ and groups, plus the Bay's warehouse-scale second-hand bookstore with DVDs, CDs and vinyl.

Gym & Fitness

Yoga & Pilates

Golf

A local 9-hole course with roll-up play and affordable green fees, plus an 18-hole mini putt on site.

Walks & Outdoor Trails

Panoramic hill tracks and flat coastal cycle paths — summit walks with Bay of Plenty views, family-friendly reserves and 20km of mountain-bike trails. Free entry; check track status after weather.

Groups & Events

Built for a crowd — paintball and laser tag, VR and escape rooms, surf lessons and golf days combine well for stag and hens weekends, team building and corporate functions, with large-scale venues a short drive away.

More Activities in Pāpāmoa

Plenty more — family playgrounds and community sports clubs, Māori cultural heritage in the Pāpāmoa Hills pā sites, mini golf and padel, plus skydiving, go-karts and Bay of Plenty winery tours a short drive away.
