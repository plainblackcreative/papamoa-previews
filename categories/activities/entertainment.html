❓ Common Questions About Activities in Pāpāmoa
What are the best things to do in Pāpāmoa? +
Pāpāmoa offers a wide range of activities. The beach is the anchor — patrolled for swimming Oct–Apr, and popular for surf casting year-round. Hakanini Surf School runs lessons and board hire at the Domain. The Cave has VR and sim racing at Ashley Place. Summerhill Golf Club has a 9-hole course. Pāpāmoa Hills is great for walking and mountain biking with panoramic Bay views. Gordon Spratt Reserve is ideal for families on foot or wheels.
Where can I learn to surf in Pāpāmoa? +
Hakanini Surf School operates from Pāpāmoa Domain, running group and private lessons for all ages, school holiday programmes and board hire from $35 half-day. They focus on ocean safety — a good choice for first-timers or those returning after a long break.
Is there a VR or gaming venue in Pāpāmoa? +
Yes. The Cave at 7/16 Ashley Place, Pāpāmoa East offers virtual reality experiences, VR escape rooms and sim racing sessions for ages 8 and up. Suitable for individuals, birthday parties and corporate groups up to 40. Book online through FareHarbor.
Where can I play golf near Pāpāmoa? +
Summerhill Golf Club at 23 Summerhill Drive is Pāpāmoa’s local 9-hole course. Green fees are around $30 for 9 holes. Call 021 051 8653 for tee times. There are also full-length courses in Tauranga nearby for those wanting 18 holes.
Can you go fishing at Pāpāmoa Beach? +
Yes — Pāpāmoa Beach is a popular surf-casting spot. The beach runs for several kilometres with multiple access points. Snapper, kahawai and trevally are the main targets depending on season and conditions. No licence is required for recreational saltwater fishing in New Zealand.
Are there walking tracks near Pāpāmoa? +
Pāpāmoa Hills Regional Park has walking and mountain biking tracks with panoramic views of the Bay of Plenty and Maketu. Entry is free. Gordon Spratt Reserve has flat, paved paths suitable for all ages including families with pushchairs. The beach itself is walkable for kilometres in either direction.
