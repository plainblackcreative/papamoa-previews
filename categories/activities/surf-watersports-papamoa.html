🚣 Kayaking & Stand-Up Paddleboarding

Kayaking & SUP at Pāpāmoa Beach

  • Pāpāmoa’s 16km coastline is well-suited to kayaking and SUP, particularly on calm mornings.
  • Conditions are generally flattest in the early morning before the sea breeze builds.
  • Multiple beach access points along Pāpāmoa Beach Road and Te Okuroa Drive.
  • No dedicated hire operator is currently confirmed within Pāpāmoa as of 2026 — bring your own gear or check listings below.
  • For open-water kayaking tours in the wider Bay of Plenty, Tauranga-based operators run coastal and harbour tours.

⛵ Boating & Charters

Boating near Pāpāmoa

  • Boat ramps: The main boat ramps in the area are at Tauranga Harbour, approximately 15–20 minutes from Pāpāmoa.
  • Fishing charters: Charter boats operating offshore in the Bay of Plenty generally depart from Tauranga Harbour. See the Fishing page for charter options.
  • Wildlife cruises: Dolphin and wildlife boat cruises operate from Tauranga Harbour. Bay of Plenty waters have abundant marine life year-round.
  • Pāpāmoa Beach itself is a surf beach with no protected harbour — not suitable for small boat launching directly from the beach.

❓ Common Questions
Where can I book surf lessons in Pāpāmoa?▼
Hakanini Surf School & Rentals operates from Pāpāmoa Domain, right by the beach. They offer group and private lessons for all ages, school holiday programmes and board hire from $35 half-day. See their full listing for contact details.
How much does surf school cost in Pāpāmoa?▼
Hakanini Surf School offers board hire from $35 half-day. Group and private lesson prices vary — check their listing for current pricing or contact them directly.
Are surf lessons in Pāpāmoa suitable for beginners?▼
Yes. Hakanini specifically caters for first-timers and beginners. Pāpāmoa Beach is a consistent beach break well-suited to learning. Instructors tailor sessions to all ability levels with a strong focus on ocean safety.
Do Pāpāmoa surf schools run holiday programmes?▼
Yes. Hakanini Surf School runs school holiday programmes. Contact them in advance — programmes fill quickly during peak holiday periods.
Can you kayak at Pāpāmoa Beach?▼
Yes. Pāpāmoa’s 16km coastline is suitable for kayaking and SUP, particularly on calm mornings. There is no confirmed dedicated kayak hire operator within Pāpāmoa as of 2026 — bring your own equipment or check Tauranga-based operators for hire options.
Are there boat tours or fishing charters near Pāpāmoa?▼
Wildlife cruises and fishing charter boats operate from Tauranga Harbour, approximately 15–20 minutes from Pāpāmoa. See the Fishing page for charter options.
