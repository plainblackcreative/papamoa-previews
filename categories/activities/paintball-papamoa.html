What to Expect

Paintball near Pāpāmoa - practical info

  • Closest venue: Paintball Extreme at Mercury Baypark, Te Maunga - approximately 15 minutes from Pāpāmoa.
  • Age: Most operators cater for ages 13 and up. Some formats (including laser tag) may suit younger players. Confirm with the operator when booking.
  • Booking: Both operators require advance bookings, especially for weekend slots and group events. Book at least a week ahead for stag/hens groups.
  • What’s included: Safety mask, paintball gun and several game modes are typically included in the base package. Additional paintballs available to purchase on the day.
  • Wear dark clothes: Paintball paint washes out of most fabrics but dark, old clothing is recommended. Both operators supply protective gear.
  • Laser tag: Available at Paintball Extreme as a lower-impact alternative - great for mixed groups or younger players.

Common Questions
Is there paintball near Pāpāmoa?▼
Yes. The closest paintball to Pāpāmoa is Paintball Extreme at Mercury Baypark, 81 Truman Road, Te Maunga - approximately 15 minutes away. They offer indoor and outdoor paintball and laser tag. Hotshots Paintball at TECT Park (off SH36, ~25–30 minutes) is another option with a natural outdoor setting.
How old do you have to be to play paintball near Pāpāmoa?▼
Most operators cater for ages 13 and up. Laser tag at Paintball Extreme may suit younger players. Confirm age requirements directly with the operator when booking, especially for mixed-age groups.
Is paintball good for a stag do near Pāpāmoa?▼
Yes. Both Paintball Extreme and Hotshots Paintball specifically cater for stag and hen parties. It’s competitive, active and needs no prior experience. See the Stag & Hens guide for more Pāpāmoa and Bay of Plenty ideas.
Can you do paintball for corporate team building near Pāpāmoa?▼
Yes. Both operators offer team building packages. Paintball builds communication, problem-solving and leadership in a competitive outdoor setting. Contact operators directly for group pricing and availability. See the Corporate Team Building page for more options.
Is there laser tag near Pāpāmoa?▼
Yes. Paintball Extreme at Mercury Baypark (approximately 15 minutes from Pāpāmoa) offers indoor laser tag alongside their paintball fields. A lower-impact alternative suitable for a wider age range.
