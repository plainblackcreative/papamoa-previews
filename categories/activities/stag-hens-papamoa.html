💼 Pāpāmoa’s most unique group experience
The Cave — VR, Sim Racing & Escape Rooms
📍 7/16 Ashley Place, Pāpāmoa East · Groups up to 40 · Ages 8+ · 0800 228 369

The Cave is one of the Bay of Plenty’s most genuinely original group experiences — and it’s right here in Pāpāmoa. Virtual reality experiences, VR escape rooms and simulator racing for groups up to 40. Not the usual pub crawl or paintball run; this is something people actually talk about afterwards. Catering options available. Contact them directly to discuss stag or hens packages and book your dates.

🏠 Where to Stay — Holiday Homes for Groups

Finding the right base in Pāpāmoa

  • Holiday homes via Airbnb & Bookabach — largest selection of group-suitable properties in Pāpāmoa. Search for properties with 4+ bedrooms and outdoor entertaining space. Use the group size filter to narrow results.
  • Beyonder — official AIMS Games accommodation manager, also manages holiday homes for general group hire. Good option for larger groups.
  • Pāpāmoa East & Golden Sands — newer area with larger homes, further from the beach but more affordable. Good for big groups.
  • Pāpāmoa Domain area — closest to the beach and to Hakanini Surf School. Best for groups who want walkable beach access.
  • Book well in advance for weekends and summer — Pāpāmoa holiday homes fill quickly, especially December–January and long weekends.

See the Holiday Homes page and Short Stays page for full options.

🍺 Food & Drinks in Pāpāmoa

Where to eat and drink with a group

  • The Island — 20 Domain Road, Pāpāmoa Beach. Pāpāmoa’s own brewpub. The largest beer garden in the Bay of Plenty, wood-fired pizza, Texas-style BBQ and own-brewed Easy Lager. Explicitly offers function hire for work shouts and group get-togethers. Phone: 07 282 8180 · theisland.nz
  • Blackberry Eatery — Pāpāmoa Plaza. Modern all-day dining, good for a group brunch or lunch. Family-friendly with a strong local following.
  • Tauranga restaurants — approximately 15–20 minutes from Pāpāmoa. The Strand, downtown Tauranga and Mount Maunganui all have a wide range of bars, restaurants and venues for larger groups.

❓ Common Questions
What are good stag do ideas near Pāpāmoa?▼
The Cave at Ashley Place in Pāpāmoa East offers VR experiences, escape rooms and sim racing for groups up to 40 — genuinely different from the usual. Paintball at Mercury Baypark (15 min) is another popular choice. Surf lessons with Hakanini, fishing charters from Tauranga Harbour, and hiring a holiday home in Pāpāmoa all work well together for a multi-day stag weekend.
What are good hens do ideas near Pāpāmoa?▼
VR experiences at The Cave (surprisingly fun for hens groups), surf lessons with Hakanini, yoga or pilates at ReDefined Wellbeing Hub or Mahana Yoga, and hot pools at Welcome Bay (15 min). Hiring a holiday home in Pāpāmoa for the group gives you a great base with beach access.
Can you hire a holiday home for a stag or hens group near Pāpāmoa?▼
Yes. Pāpāmoa has a wide range of holiday homes on Airbnb and Bookabach, including larger properties with multiple bedrooms and outdoor entertaining space. See the Holiday Homes page for options.
How many people does The Cave accommodate for a stag or hens group?▼
The Cave at 7/16 Ashley Place, Pāpāmoa East caters for groups up to 40 people with VR experiences, escape rooms and sim racing. Catering options available. Call 0800 228 369 to discuss your group’s requirements and book dates.
