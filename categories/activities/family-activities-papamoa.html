👪 Family Activities · Pāpāmoa

Family Activities in Pāpāmoa

Pāpāmoa is one of New Zealand’s most family-friendly suburbs — a patrolled beach, VR entertainment, surf lessons, playgrounds and free outdoor spaces.

🏄 Top Family Activities in Pāpāmoa

Pāpāmoa is one of New Zealand’s most family-friendly communities — a fast-growing coastal suburb with a long patrolled beach, multiple parks and playgrounds, a surf club with junior programmes, and a growing range of indoor activities. Most major attractions are within a 10-minute drive of anywhere in the suburb.

🎮
The Cave Pāpāmoa — VR & Sim Racing
📍 7/16 Ashley Place, Pāpāmoa East · Ages 8+

Pāpāmoa’s best wet-day option for families with older children — virtual reality experiences, VR escape rooms and sim racing at Ashley Place. Birthday parties catered for, groups up to 40. Ideal for ages 8 and up. Book online through FareHarbor.

Hakanini Surf School
📍 Pāpāmoa Domain · Ages 5+ for Junior Surf

Surf lessons and board hire for all ages at Pāpāmoa Domain. School holiday programmes run throughout summer. Board hire from $35 half-day. Pāpāmoa Surf Life Saving Club also runs a Junior Surf Programme for ages 5–13 from the same beach reserve.

Gordon Spratt Reserve

Flat sealed cycle and walking paths, playground, skate park, sports fields and a wetland walk — all free. Suitable for all ages including toddlers and pushchairs. Connected to Pāpāmoa library. See the Gordon Spratt Reserve guide.

Pāpāmoa Beach

16km of white sand, patrolled Oct–Apr. Swim between the flags, build sandcastles, fish from the shore or simply walk the length of the beach. The Domain access is the main patrolled swimming zone.

❓ Common Questions
What can families do in Pāpāmoa?+
Pāpāmoa offers a strong range of family activities: The Cave (VR and sim racing), Hakanini Surf School, Gordon Spratt Reserve (playgrounds, cycle paths), Pāpāmoa Hills (walking), the patrolled beach, parks and playgrounds across the suburb, and mini putt at Summerhill Golf Club.
What is there to do in Pāpāmoa on a rainy day?+
The Cave at Ashley Place is Pāpāmoa’s best wet-day option — VR experiences and sim racing for ages 8 and up. Xanadu Book Exchange at 2C Ashley Place is a beloved second-hand bookshop. Pāpāmoa Library at 15 Gravatt Road offers story time and events.
Where are the playgrounds in Pāpāmoa?+
Pāpāmoa has multiple playgrounds and parks across the suburb. Gordon Spratt Reserve has a playground, skate park and flat paths. See the Parks & Playgrounds community guide for a full list of locations.
