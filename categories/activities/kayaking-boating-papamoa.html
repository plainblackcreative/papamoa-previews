🚣 Kayaking & Paddleboarding at Pāpāmoa

Pāpāmoa’s 16km coastline is an excellent environment for kayaking and stand-up paddleboarding (SUP). Conditions are generally calmer in the morning. The beach has multiple access points and the flat water on calm days is well-suited to beginners. No dedicated hire operator is currently confirmed within Pāpāmoa itself — check listings below for updates.

❓ Common Questions
Can you kayak at Pāpāmoa Beach?+
Yes — Pāpāmoa’s coastline is suitable for kayaking and SUP, particularly on calm mornings. There is no confirmed hire operator within Pāpāmoa as of 2026. Bring your own equipment or check Tauranga-based operators for hire options.
Are there boat tours near Pāpāmoa?+
Wildlife cruises and dolphin-watching boat tours operate from Tauranga Harbour, about 20 minutes from Pāpāmoa. Glow worm kayak tours depart from Waimarino in Tauranga. Fishing charters also run from the harbour.
