🌎 Walks & Trails · Pāpāmoa

Walks & Trails Near Pāpāmoa

Pāpāmoa Hills Regional Park, Gordon Spratt Reserve and 16 kilometres of beach — walking options for all ages and abilities.

⛰ Pāpāmoa Hills Regional Park
⛰
Pāpāmoa Hills Regional Park
📍 Pāpāmoa Hills · Walking & Mountain Biking

Pāpāmoa’s most scenic walking destination — tracks wind through a landscape containing seven historical Māori pā sites (fortified settlements) visible along the route. The main summit track takes approximately 45 minutes return and offers 360° views of the Bay of Plenty from Mount Maunganui to Makētu. Mountain biking tracks also available. Free entry. Note: partially reopened April 2026 after weather damage — check boprc.govt.nz for current track status before visiting.

🌎 Gordon Spratt Reserve
🌎
Gordon Spratt Reserve
📍 Gordon Spratt Reserve, Pāpāmoa · Flat walking & cycling

Pāpāmoa’s family-friendly reserve with flat, paved walking and cycling paths suitable for all ages including pushchairs and young children. Sports fields, a skate park, a wetland walk and playground are also on site. Connects to the Pāpāmoa library at 15 Gravatt Road. Free entry and parking.

🏄 Pāpāmoa Beach Walk

Pāpāmoa Beach runs for 16 kilometres and is walkable for its entire length. The beach is patrolled by surf lifeguards Oct–Apr at the Domain reserve area. Early morning walks along the beach at sunrise are a local favourite. Dog-friendly sections are available — see the dogs in Pāpāmoa guide for beach rules.

❓ Common Questions
Are there walking tracks near Pāpāmoa?+
Yes. Pāpāmoa Hills Regional Park has walking and MTB tracks with panoramic Bay of Plenty views (partially reopened April 2026 after weather closures — check current status). Gordon Spratt Reserve has flat paths ideal for families. The beach itself is walkable for 16 kilometres.
Is Pāpāmoa Hills open?+
Pāpāmoa Hills Regional Park was partially reopened in April 2026 following severe weather that caused significant landslides across the park in January 2026. Check boprc.govt.nz for current track status before visiting.
Is the walk at Pāpāmoa Hills suitable for children?+
The main summit track is approximately 45 minutes return and is moderate grade — steep in parts. Suitable for children who are comfortable walkers. Closed footwear and water recommended. Gordon Spratt Reserve is the better option for younger children or pushchairs.
