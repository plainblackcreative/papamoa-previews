Takeaways in Pāpāmoa — common questions

Pāpāmoa has takeaway options across most cuisines: pizza (Pizza Pundits, Hell, Domino’s, Pizza Hut, Beach Pizza), Indian (Mumbai Masala, two Great Spice locations), Thai (Thai Thani), Chinese (Canton Kitchen, Oceanview), sushi and Japanese (Bay Sushi, Hello Sushi, Smile Sushi, Meltingpot), fish & chips (Aunty’s Sea Fresh, Fishop, Oceanview), burgers (The Wagon, BurgerFuel, McD’s, KFC), Mexican (Ruby’s Tacos), Turkish (Turkish To Go), and bakeries. Most are accessible via DoorDash, Uber Eats or Delivereasy.
Yes. DoorDash, Uber Eats and Delivereasy all operate in Pāpāmoa. Highest-rated delivery options include Hello Sushi (4.9★ DoorDash), Ruby’s Tacos (4.9★ Uber Eats) and Turkish To Go (4.7★ DoorDash, 4.6★ Uber Eats from 1,500+ ratings). The Pizza Pundits delivers via all three platforms.
The Pizza Pundits at 1/898 Pāpāmoa Beach Road holds a 4.9-star TripAdvisor rating — one of the highest-rated food businesses in Pāpāmoa. For delivery specifically, Hello Sushi (4.9★) and Turkish To Go (4.7★ DoorDash, 1,500+ Uber Eats reviews) are standout options.
The Pizza Pundits is open 7 nights from 4:30pm, till 10pm on Fridays and Saturdays. Domino’s at 38 Gravatt Road is open daily till 10:30pm. McDonald’s and KFC are open daily. Turkish To Go is open daily till 8:30pm. Great Spice Plaza and Great Spice East both serve till 10pm.
