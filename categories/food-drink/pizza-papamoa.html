The Pizza Pundits ◆ Silver
1/898 Pāpāmoa Beach Road 7 nights, 4:30pm–9pm (Fri–Sat till 10pm) DoorDash, Uber Eats, Delivereasy
4.9 TripAdvisor - 58 reviews

Pāpāmoa’s top-rated pizza kitchen at 1/898 Pāpāmoa Beach Road, operating from the former Pizza Library location. A quirky pizzeria making large handcrafted pizzas seven nights a week. The standout is the house-made gluten-free base - reviewers specifically praise the texture, noting it far surpasses standard GF bases available elsewhere. Menu covers vegetarian, classic and gourmet pizzas alongside sides and drinks. Supports local youth sports teams. Delivery via DoorDash, Uber Eats and Delivereasy. Dine-in also available.

Beach Pizza Pāpāmoa
11/32 Turiwhatu Street, Pāpāmoa 07 542 0666

A local independent pizza shop on Turiwhatu Street with a vegan and non-vegan menu. Standout flavours include peri peri paneer and apricot chicken, both consistently praised for generous toppings and bold flavours. Good for groups and family orders. Delivery via Uber Eats and Foodhub. Order online at beachpizzapapamoa.co.nz.

Basilico - Neapolitan Woodfire Pizza
520 Gloucester Road, Pāpāmoa Beach Tue–Sun from 10am–11am, Mon closed 07 572 2996

Authentic Italian woodfire pizzeria with 72-hour dough fermentation. Italian chef Michele, ingredients from Italy including burning woods, locally made mozzarella from a Nelson artisan. Neapolitan-style thin base with crispy bottom and slightly charred crust. Not a takeaway chain - the most artisan pizza option in Pāpāmoa. Order online via bopple.app/basilico. Also serves pasta, arancini and tiramisu.
