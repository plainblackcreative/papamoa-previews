Function Venues in Pāpāmoa — FAQs

What function venues are in Pāpāmoa?

PAP House on Pāpāmoa Beach Road is a restaurant and bar open 7 days with indoor and outdoor spaces suited to groups. BeachBar at 559 Pāpāmoa Beach Road is the area’s only beachfront venue with bookable bistro space. Lantern at 20 Domain Road takes group dining reservations.

Where can I hold a birthday party or event in Pāpāmoa?

For food and drink events: PAP House, BeachBar and Lantern all accommodate groups. For activity-based birthdays, The Cave at Ashley Place offers VR and sim racing for ages 10+. Pacific Park has group lodges for larger stay-based events.

Is BeachBar Pāpāmoa available for private hire?

Contact BeachBar directly at beachbar.co.nz to discuss private hire and function options. The bistro side operates with bookings, while the bar area is a casual walk-in space.
