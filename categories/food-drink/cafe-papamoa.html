Tide & Roast Pāpāmoa ★ Spotlight
📍 Beach Road, Pāpāmoa 🕐 Daily 7am–3pm ✔ Specialty Coffee & Brunch

Tide & Roast is Pāpāmoa's most talked-about cafe, known for its single-origin espresso programme and a brunch menu that takes seasonal produce seriously. They source beans directly from a Tauranga roastery and the baristas are trained to competition level. The food is creative without being pretentious -- think eggs done properly, house-made granola, and weekend specials that sell out fast. The fitout is relaxed and airy, and the outdoor tables fill quickly on fine mornings. Worth the wait.

The Shoreline Cafe Pāpāmoa
📍 Domain Road, Pāpāmoa 🕐 Mon–Sat 7am–4pm, Sun 8am–3pm

The Shoreline Cafe is a friendly neighbourhood spot with consistently good flat whites and a reliable cabinet food selection. It is popular with the morning school run crowd and the late-morning regulars who come for a longer stay. Gluten-free and dairy-free options are clearly marked. No bookings required -- walk-ins only.
