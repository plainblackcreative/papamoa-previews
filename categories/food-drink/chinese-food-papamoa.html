Chinese & Asian Food in Pāpāmoa
Canton Kitchen
18 Gravatt Road, Pāpāmoa Beach Tue–Sun 5:15pm–8:15pm, Mon closed

Pāpāmoa’s dedicated Cantonese-style Chinese takeaway on Gravatt Road. The menu covers the classics: sweet and sour pork, chicken chow mein, combination fried rice, wontons, spring rolls and more. Rated 4.3 stars. Evening only - open from 5:15pm Tuesday to Sunday. Online ordering available via their TuckerFox page. Also operates as a smorgasbord - see the All You Can Eat page for details.

Order Online → Website →
Oceanview Takeaways
548 Pāpāmoa Beach Road, Pāpāmoa Beach Wed–Sun 11:30am–8pm, Mon–Tue 3:30pm–8pm

A combined Chinese-style Asian food and fish & chips takeaway at the eastern end of Pāpāmoa Beach Road. Menu covers both Asian dishes (wontons, chicken nuggets, hot dogs, potato fritters) and classic fish & chips. Open most days from either 11:30am or 3:30pm. Good option if you’re at the beach end of Pāpāmoa and want takeaway without driving to Fashion Island. Online ordering available.

Order Online → Website →
Lantern
20 Domain Road, Pāpāmoa 3118 Tue–Sun 12pm–late, Mon closed 022 121 1755 hello@lantern.nz

Southeast Asian restaurant and bar at Domain Road, close to The Island brewpub. The menu draws broadly from Southeast Asian cuisines - not strictly Chinese, but includes dishes that will be familiar to those seeking Asian dining. Table reservations available online. Gift cards sold. Lantern also has a dedicated vegan Chef’s Selection menu with plant-based Southeast Asian dishes - see the Vegan page for more. Open Tuesday to Sunday from noon.
