❓ Common Questions
What are the best restaurants in Pāpāmoa? ▼
Pāpāmoa has a strong and growing dining scene. The Wagon on Toorea Street is known for gourmet burgers and craft cocktails. PAP House on Pāpāmoa Beach Road is a dog-friendly restaurant and bar open seven days. The Pizza Pundits holds a 4.9-star TripAdvisor rating for gourmet pizza. Mumbai Masala at Fashion Island is the top-rated Indian restaurant with 4.5 stars from 387 Google reviews. Blackberry Eatery is the go-to brunch cafe.
Where can I get coffee and brunch in Pāpāmoa? ▼
Blackberry Eatery is Pāpāmoa’s most talked-about brunch spot, known for generous portions and quality coffee. Fashion Island and Pāpāmoa Plaza both have clusters of cafes. For specialty coffee, several independent cafes operate across the suburb — see the Cafes sub-category for the current list.
Does Pāpāmoa have bars or pubs? ▼
Yes. PAP House on Pāpāmoa Beach Road is open seven days as a restaurant and bar. The Wagon is fully licensed with craft cocktails and shared plates. Several other venues along the beach strip also offer evening drinks. See the Bars & Pubs sub-category for the full list.
Where can I get pizza delivered in Pāpāmoa? ▼
The Pizza Pundits at 1/898 Pāpāmoa Beach Road delivers via DoorDash, Uber Eats and Delivereasy, open seven nights from 4:30pm. They offer house-made gluten-free bases and are rated 4.9 stars on TripAdvisor.
Where is the best Indian restaurant in Pāpāmoa? ▼
Mumbai Masala at Fashion Island (30 Gravatt Road) is Pāpāmoa’s most established Indian restaurant with a 4.5-star Google rating from 387 reviews. They serve curries, tandoor dishes, biryanis and a strong vegetarian and vegan menu. BYO, open from 4:30pm.
Is there a supermarket in Pāpāmoa East? ▼
Yes. New World The Sands opened in late 2024 at The Sands town centre in Pāpāmoa East (Golden Sands). It is one of the largest new supermarkets in the Bay of Plenty with in-store butchers, bakers and fishmongers. Countdown Pāpāmoa Plaza serves the western end of Pāpāmoa.
