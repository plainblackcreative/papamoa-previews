Eating out in Pāpāmoa — common questions

Pāpāmoa has a strong and growing dining scene. The Wagon on Toorea Street is known for gourmet burgers and craft cocktails. PAP House on Pāpāmoa Beach Road is a dog-friendly restaurant and bar open seven days. The Pizza Pundits holds a 4.9-star TripAdvisor rating. Mumbai Masala at Fashion Island is the top-rated Indian restaurant. Blackberry Eatery is the go-to brunch cafe. See the Restaurants page for all listings.
Blackberry Eatery is Pāpāmoa’s most talked-about brunch spot, known for generous portions and quality coffee. Fashion Island and Pāpāmoa Plaza both have clusters of cafes. See the Cafes page for current listings.
Yes. PAP House on Pāpāmoa Beach Road is open seven days as a restaurant and bar. The Wagon is fully licensed with craft cocktails and shared plates. See the Bars & Pubs page for the full list.
The Pizza Pundits at 1/898 Pāpāmoa Beach Road delivers via DoorDash, Uber Eats and Delivereasy. Open seven nights from 4:30pm. House-made gluten-free bases available. See the Pizza page.
Mumbai Masala at Fashion Island (30 Gravatt Road) is Pāpāmoa’s most established Indian restaurant. Curries, tandoor, biryanis and a strong vegetarian and vegan menu. BYO, open from 4:30pm. See the Indian Restaurants page.
Yes. Thai Thani is at 30 Gravatt Road, Fashion Island. Over 35 mains, licensed and BYO wine, open Tuesday to Sunday. See the Thai Food page.
Lantern at 20 Domain Road has a dedicated vegan Chef’s Selection menu. Mumbai Masala and Thai Thani also have strong plant-based options. See the Vegan page.
Yes. New World The Sands opened in late 2024 at The Sands town centre in Pāpāmoa East. Woolworths Pāpāmoa Plaza serves the western end. See the Supermarkets page.
Yes. McDonald’s Pāpāmoa is at 7 Gravatt Road near Fashion Island, with a drive-thru. KFC also operates in Pāpāmoa. See the Fast Food page.
Ruby’s Tacos & Tequila on Domain Road is open 7 days from 12pm. Pablo is a Latin-inspired restaurant open Tuesday to Sunday. See the Mexican Food page.

🍕 Eating out in Pāpāmoa

  • Fashion Island has the highest concentration of restaurants
  • Pāpāmoa Plaza has cafes, takeaways and a supermarket
  • BYO is common — bring your own wine to many local restaurants
  • Delivery available via DoorDash, Uber Eats and Delivereasy
  • Dog-friendly options including several outdoor dining areas
  • Hours vary — many restaurants open from 4:30pm only
  • New World The Sands opened Pāpāmoa East in late 2024
