The Wagon

🍕 Gourmet Burgers & Bar · 8 Toorea Street, Pāpāmoa

The Wagon is Pāpāmoa’s standout gourmet burger and bar. Everything is made from fresh ingredients — no frozen patties — and the drinks list runs to craft cocktails, local wines and an NZ-focused beer selection. Fully licensed, dine-in or takeaway. Relaxed atmosphere with a reputation for quality that draws regulars from across Tauranga.

The Pizza Pundits

🍕 Gourmet Pizza · 1/898 Pāpāmoa Beach Road

❓ Common Questions About Eating in Pāpāmoa
Pāpāmoa has a strong and growing dining scene. The Wagon on Toorea Street is known for gourmet burgers and craft cocktails. PAP House on Pāpāmoa Beach Road is a dog-friendly restaurant and bar open seven days. The Pizza Pundits holds a 4.9-star TripAdvisor rating for gourmet pizza. Mumbai Masala at Fashion Island is the top-rated Indian restaurant with 4.5 stars from 387 Google reviews. Blackberry Eatery is the go-to brunch cafe.
Blackberry Eatery is Pāpāmoa’s most talked-about brunch spot, known for generous portions and quality coffee. Fashion Island and Pāpāmoa Plaza both have clusters of cafes. For specialty coffee, several independent cafes operate across the suburb — see the Cafes sub-category for the current list.
Yes. PAP House on Pāpāmoa Beach Road is open seven days as a restaurant and bar. The Wagon is fully licensed with craft cocktails and shared plates. Several other venues along the beach strip also offer evening drinks. See the Bars & Pubs sub-category for the full list.
The Pizza Pundits at 1/898 Pāpāmoa Beach Road delivers via DoorDash, Uber Eats and Delivereasy, open seven nights from 4:30pm. They offer house-made gluten-free bases and are rated 4.9 stars on TripAdvisor.
Mumbai Masala at Fashion Island (30 Gravatt Road) is Pāpāmoa’s most established Indian restaurant with a 4.5-star Google rating from 387 reviews. They serve curries, tandoor dishes, biryanis and a strong vegetarian and vegan menu. BYO, open from 4:30pm.
Yes. Thai Thani is at 30 Gravatt Road, Fashion Island. They serve authentic Thai food with over 35 mains, including curries, pad thai and stir-fries. Fully licensed and BYO wine. Open Tuesday to Sunday for lunch and dinner. Order online via ordermeal.co.nz.
Yes. New World The Sands opened in late 2024 at The Sands town centre in Pāpāmoa East. It is one of the largest new supermarkets in the Bay of Plenty with in-store butchers, bakers and fishmongers. Woolworths Pāpāmoa Plaza serves the western end of Pāpāmoa.
Lantern at 20 Domain Road has a dedicated vegan Chef’s Selection menu with Southeast Asian plant-based dishes. Mumbai Masala at Fashion Island has an extensive vegan and vegetarian range. Thai Thani also caters to vegan diners. See the Vegan & Plant-Based page for more detail.
Ruby’s Tacos & Tequila on Domain Road is open 7 days from 12pm. Pablo is a Latin-inspired restaurant open Tuesday to Sunday from 11am with a tapas-style menu. See the Mexican Food page.
Yes. McDonald’s Pāpāmoa is at 7 Gravatt Road near Fashion Island, with a drive-thru. KFC also operates in Pāpāmoa. See the Fast Food & Drive-Thru page for addresses and hours.

🍕 Eating out in Pāpāmoa

  • Fashion Island has the highest concentration of restaurants
  • Pāpāmoa Plaza has cafes, takeaways and a supermarket
  • BYO is common — bring your own wine to many local restaurants
  • Delivery available via DoorDash, Uber Eats and Delivereasy
  • Dog-friendly options include PAP House and several outdoor areas
  • Hours vary — many restaurants open from 4:30pm only
  • Brunch is strong — Pāpāmoa has several well-regarded cafe options
  • New World The Sands opened Pāpāmoa East in late 2024
