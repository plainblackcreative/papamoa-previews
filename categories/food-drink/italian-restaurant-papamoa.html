Italian Food in Pāpāmoa
Basilico Authentic Italian Pizza & Deli
520 Gloucester Road, Pāpāmoa Beach Tue–Thu 10am–8:30pm, Fri 10am–9pm, Sat–Sun 11am–9pm/8:30pm, Mon closed 07 572 2996

Pāpāmoa’s only dedicated Italian restaurant and pizzeria, off Sandhurst Drive. Chef Michele is Italian, as are the raw materials - including the burning woods used in cooking. The dough rests for a minimum of 72 hours to develop flavour, structure and digestibility. The mozzarella and mascarpone are made locally by an Italian artisan in Nelson, making the pizzas and tiramisu exceptional by local standards. Standout dishes: Margherita, Tartufata (truffle and porcini cream base), arancini mushroom truffle, and tiramisu. Vegetarian and vegan options available. Also serves pasta and Italian deli items. Sister venue Rustica is in Mount Maunganui at 10 Adams Avenue. Online ordering for pickup via bopple.app/basilico.
