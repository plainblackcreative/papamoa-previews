Basilico is Pāpāmoa’s Italian-style pizzeria at 520 Gloucester Road. Italian Food in Pāpāmoa — Frequently Asked Questions

Is there an Italian restaurant in Pāpāmoa?

Yes. Basilico at 520 Gloucester Road is Pāpāmoa’s dedicated Italian-style pizzeria and deli. They use a 72-hour dough fermentation process and serve pizza, pasta and Italian deli items. Order online via bopple.app/basilico.

What makes Basilico’s pizza different?

Basilico uses a Neapolitan-method pizza dough that rests for a minimum of 72 hours. This extended fermentation develops deeper flavour and improves the digestibility of the crust. They describe it as the minimum time needed to reach proper dough maturation.

Can I order Basilico pizza online?

Yes. Order via bopple.app/basilico. Their Pāpāmoa location is at 520 Gloucester Road, Pāpāmoa Beach. Their Mount Maunganui sister venue, Rustica, is at 10 Adams Avenue.
