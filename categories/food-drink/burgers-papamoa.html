Burgers in Pāpāmoa — Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the best burger in Pāpāmoa?

The Wagon at 8 Toorea Street is Pāpāmoa’s most-loved gourmet burger spot, known for fresh, handcrafted burgers and a fully licensed bar. BurgerFuel at Fashion Island offers the well-known NZ chain experience with natural ingredients and gluten-free bun options.

Can I get a burger delivered in Pāpāmoa?

Yes. BurgerFuel Pāpāmoa delivers via Uber Eats. The Wagon offers online ordering through ordermeal.co.nz for collection — confirm delivery availability directly with the venue.

Is The Wagon in Pāpāmoa licensed?

Yes. The Wagon is fully licensed and serves craft cocktails, local wines and NZ craft beers alongside their food menu. Open Tuesday to Sunday from 11am.

Does Pāpāmoa have a BurgerFuel?

Yes. BurgerFuel Pāpāmoa is located at Fashion Island, T2/42 Gravatt Road, and has been open since 2018. Open daily from 10am, with dine-in and takeaway available.
