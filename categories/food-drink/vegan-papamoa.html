Best Vegan Dining in Pāpāmoa
Vegan menu
Lantern - Pāpāmoa
20 Domain Road, Pāpāmoa Beach Tue–Sun, 12pm–late 022 121 1755
Vegan Chef’s Selection menu includes Rendang Vegetables · Balinese Salad · Silken Tofu · Yellow Curry · Charred Edamame · Seasonal Greens

Lantern is Pāpāmoa’s standout option for plant-based dining. The Southeast Asian restaurant and bar at 20 Domain Road offers a dedicated vegan Chef’s Selection menu alongside their standard menu - not just one or two token dishes, but a proper sharing menu designed for vegan diners. The food is inspired by Southeast Asian cuisine in a lush garden setting. 4.8 stars from 552 reviews. Open Tuesday to Sunday from 12pm. Book at lantern.nz.
