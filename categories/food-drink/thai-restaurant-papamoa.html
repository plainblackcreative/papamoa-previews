Pāpāmoa’s established Thai restaurant at Fashion Island, 30 Gravatt Road. The owners lived in Thailand for nine years and the menu reflects that direct experience — over 35 mains covering curries (green, red, massaman, panang), stir-fries, pad thai, soups, salads and noodle dishes. Vegetarian and vegan options available. Fully licensed with bar service and BYO wine — a combination that makes it well-suited to group dinners and celebrations. Dine-in with bookings recommended, especially weekends. Online ordering via ordermeal.co.nz for pickup. A consistent long-term favourite in Pāpāmoa.
