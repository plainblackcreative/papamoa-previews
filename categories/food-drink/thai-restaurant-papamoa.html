Thai Food in Pāpāmoa — FAQs

Is there a Thai restaurant in Pāpāmoa?

Yes. Thai Thani is at 30 Gravatt Road, Fashion Island. They serve authentic Thai food with over 35 mains on the menu. Open Tuesday to Sunday for lunch and dinner. BYO wine. Online ordering via ordermeal.co.nz.

What are Thai Thani’s opening hours?

Closed Monday. Tuesday to Thursday and Sunday: 12pm–2:30pm and 5pm–9pm. Friday and Saturday: 12pm–2:30pm and 5pm–9:15pm. Phone 07 574 9391 for bookings.

Can I get Thai food delivered in Pāpāmoa?

Thai Thani offers online ordering via ordermeal.co.nz. Confirm delivery availability when ordering, as takeaway and dine-in are their primary service modes.

Is Thai Thani Pāpāmoa BYO?

Yes. Thai Thani is fully licensed and also allows BYO wine at Fashion Island, 30 Gravatt Road.
