Reference

Key Principles

Emails get shorter each time

Day 3 is your fullest email. Day 6 is half the length. Day 10 is the shortest. Respect their time and inbox fatigue.

Always pair with a same-day call

Email + phone on the same day dramatically increases reply rate. The email primes them; the call closes the loop.

Never price in the first DM or email

Lead with value. The preview does the selling. Pricing conversations happen after they've seen what the listing looks like.

Gold scarcity is real — use it honestly

Only mention the Gold Spotlight limit when it's genuinely relevant. One per sub-category is the fact. Don't manufacture urgency where none exists.

TaurangaNZ.info is proof, not pitch

Drop the network reference once — in the signature or a PS. Don't build a case around it. Let it land quietly.
