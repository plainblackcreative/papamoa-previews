Key Principles
🖼

The mockup is your tipping point

Seeing their own business name on a professional listing is often what tips a "maybe" into a "yes." Build one whenever you can before sending email 1.

⏱

Timing matters

Day 3, day 6, day 10. Any sooner feels desperate. Any later loses momentum. Stick to the schedule and always send in the morning.

📉

Emails get shorter each time

Email 1 is your most detailed. Email 2 is half the length. Email 3 is shorter still. Shrinking length signals respect for their time.

🔁

Always follow up by phone too

A call on the same day as each email doubles your response rate. The email primes them, the call closes. They work together.

🛡

The guarantee closes deals

Save the ROI guarantee for hesitant prospects, not as an opener. It lands much harder when it comes after you've built some trust.

📋

Track everything in a simple sheet

Date of first contact, email 1, email 2, email 3, response, outcome. You will start to see patterns in which industries respond fastest.
