Reference

Key Principles

Emails get shorter each time

Day 0 and Day 3 are your fullest emails. Day 6 is half the length. Day 10 is the shortest. Respect their time and inbox fatigue.

Always pair with a same-day call

Email + phone on the same day dramatically increases reply rate. The email primes them; the call closes the loop.

Never price in the first DM or email

Lead with value. The hub page does the selling. Pricing conversations happen after they've seen what they'd actually get.

One link, one job

Every email drives to a single CTA: the Get-listed hub at list-with-us.html. Don't scatter links - give them one clear next step.
