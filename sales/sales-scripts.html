Cold Call Script

📞
Phone Script
Service businesses, tradies, clinics, accountants, real estate · Target: under 60 seconds · Tone: friendly local, not salesperson
The OpeningPhone Script
"Hi [Name], it's Jayden here. I'm local, just based here in Pāpāmoa. Not a telemarketer, I promise. Do you have 30 seconds?" Wait. If hesitant: "I'll be really quick."
The HookPhone Script
"I've just launched a local directory called papamoa.info. And I mean genuinely local — every business on it is Papamoa-owned or run by someone who actually lives here. It's live now if you want to check it out. The reason I'm calling you specifically is that I'm only listing one Gold Spotlight per sub-category, and I wanted to give [Business Name] first option on [their category] before I move on."
Gold Spotlight scarcity is the hook. One per sub-category, genuinely true. Don't use this angle for Silver-only prospects.
The Credibility BridgePhone Script
"We're backed by TaurangaNZ.info, which has been running since 2005, so there's real domain authority and SEO behind it from day one. You're not taking a punt on something new — you're getting in first on something with solid backing behind it."
The Permission AskPhone Script
"I don't want to take up your time right now. Can I send you two simple options? It's a two-minute read, no obligation at all." Get their preferred contact: email, text, or DM.
The ClosePhone Script
"Perfect. I'll send that through right now. Could you take a quick look by [Friday / end of week]? I'll give you a call back [specific day] to answer any questions." Confirm the callback day and time before hanging up. "Brilliant, thanks [Name]. Email is on its way. Enjoy your day!"
Always confirm a specific callback day. Without it, your email is junk mail. With it, it's a to-do item.

Objection Handling

🛡
Objection Handling
Common responses and how to handle them
Objection Response
"How much does it cost?" Silver is $599+GST per year, Gold Spotlight is $1,199+GST per year. Before we talk pricing though — can I send you a free preview so you can see what your listing would actually look like? It's easier to judge the value when you can see it.
"I'm already on Google / Yellow Pages" Those are great for general visibility — but Papamoa.info is the only directory built just for Pāpāmoa. When someone searches "[sub-category] Pāpāmoa," this is the site with 20 years of local domain authority behind it. It complements Google, it doesn't replace it.
"I don't have time to manage it" You don't have to manage anything. We research and write your listing from your website and socials. You review a draft, give the go-ahead, and it's live. Takes you maybe 10 minutes total.
"Not in my budget right now" Fair enough — Silver works out to about $11.50 a week. And every listing comes with a 2027 ROI guarantee: if we can't show a clear return from your 2026 listing, your 2027 renewal is free. It's a pretty low-risk entry point.
"I already have enough customers" That's a great position to be in. The other angle worth thinking about is Pāpāmoa's growth — 37,800 residents and growing fast. New arrivals don't have established supplier relationships. A listing now builds the habit of locals finding you there.
"Send me something in writing" Absolutely — I'll send you a personalised preview of what your listing would look like, plus a one-pager with the pricing and guarantees. What's the best email?
"Someone else handles our marketing" No problem — if you can copy them in, I'm happy to send the preview directly to them. Or I can send it to you and you forward it on. What works best?

Key Principles

📍
Key Principles
The rules that keep every outreach consistent and honest
1
Lead with the live site A live URL proves you're real. Drop "papamoa.info is live now" early. It ends the "is this legit?" question instantly.
2
One Gold Spotlight per sub-category is your strongest tool Say it early and let it do the work. Genuine scarcity, no pressure tactics needed. Never apply it to Silver — Silver has no limit.
3
Genuinely local is the hook Pāpāmoa locals are proud of their community. Being part of something built for them, by one of them, matters more than price.
4
Always lock in a callback day Without a specific day, your email is junk mail. With one, it's a to-do item. Confirm it before you hang up.
5
Never price in the first DM Your only goal is permission to send the overview. Price conversations belong in the follow-up call.
6
TaurangaNZ.info is proof, not pitch Drop it once to answer the credibility question, then move on. The story is papamoa.info.
