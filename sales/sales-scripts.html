Cold Call Script
📞

Phone Script

Service businesses, tradies, clinics, accountants, real estate

Target talk time: Under 60 seconds Tone: friendly local, not salesperson
The Opening
"Hi [Name], it's Jayden here. I'm local, just based here in Pāpāmoa. Not a telemarketer, I promise. Do you have 30 seconds?"

Wait. If hesitant: "I'll be really quick."

The Hook
"I've just launched a local directory called papamoa.info. And I mean genuinely local. Every business on it is Papamoa-owned or run by someone who actually lives here. It's live now if you want to check it out.

The reason I'm calling you specifically is that I'm only listing One Gold Spotlight per sub-category, and I wanted to give [Business Name] first option on [their category] before I move on."
The Credibility Bridge
"We're backed by TaurangaNZ.info, which has been running since 2005, so there's real domain authority and SEO behind it from day one. You're not taking a punt on something new, you're getting in first on something with solid backing behind it."
The Permission Ask
"I don't want to take up your time right now. Can I send you two simple options? It's a two-minute read, no obligation at all."

Get their preferred contact: email, text, or DM.

The Close
"Perfect. I'll send that through right now. Could you take a quick look by [Friday / end of week]? I'll give you a call back [specific day] to answer any questions."

Confirm the callback day and time before hanging up.

"Brilliant, thanks [Name]. Email is on its way. Enjoy your day!"

Objection Handling

Common responses and how to handle them

"Not interested" / "We already do marketing"
"Totally fair. This works alongside whatever you're already doing. It's purely about local visibility when someone in Pāpāmoa searches for [their service]. And because it's sub-category exclusive, if you're not on it, your local competitor will be. Can I still send it? Worst case, you delete it."
"How much is it?"
"Two options in the email. Starts from $599 plus GST. Let me send it through so you can see exactly what you'd get, and I'll call back to chat it through. If the numbers don't stack up for you, no hard feelings."
"Just tell me now"
"Sure. Silver at $599 gets you a full SEO-optimised listing. Gold at $1,200 puts you in the featured Spotlight at the top of your category, locked in for 12 months. The email has it all laid out. Can I send it and call you back [specific day]?"
"I'm busy right now"
"Perfect timing then. Give me your email, I'll send it now, and I'll call you back [day]. Two seconds."
"Send it to info@..."
"Done. And what's the best number for you when I follow up on [day]?"
"Why should I trust a new site?"
"Fair question. Two reasons. First, it's backed by TaurangaNZ.info which has been running since 2005, so the SEO foundation is already there. Second, being first is the actual advantage. You lock in the top spot in your category before anyone else can. Early movers always win on directories."

Key Principles
📍

Lead with the live site

A live URL proves you're real. Drop "papamoa.info is live now" early. It ends the "is this legit?" question instantly.

🏆

One per category is your strongest tool

Say it early and let it do the work. It creates genuine scarcity without any pressure tactics needed.

🤝

Genuinely local is the hook

Pāpāmoa locals are proud of their community. Being part of something built for them, by one of them, matters more than price.

🗓

Always lock in a callback day

Without a specific day, your email is junk mail. With one, it's a to-do item. Confirm it before you hang up.

✉️

Never price in the first DM

Your only goal is permission to send the overview. Price conversations belong in the follow-up call.

🌐

TaurangaNZ.info is proof, not pitch

Drop it once to answer the credibility question, then move on. The story is papamoa.info.
