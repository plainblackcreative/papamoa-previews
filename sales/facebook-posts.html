The Māori history of Pāpāmoa 🌿 Before the subdivisions and beach houses, this land was home to Ngā Pōtiki and Ngāi Te Rangi — two of the principal iwi of Tauranga Moana. The name Pāpāmoa comes from the flat pumice plain (papa = flat land, moa = pumice/extinct bird). Tamapahore Marae remains a living cultural institution at the heart of the community. We've published a respectful overview of the history: papamoa.info/articles/maori-history #Papamoa #MaoriHistory #NgaPotiki #NgaiTeRangi #TaurangaMoana #Aotearoa
