Common questions

Frequently asked questions

How quickly can my menu be live?
Send us your current menu (PDF, photo, or link) and we'll have it built and live within 48 hours. You can then make changes yourself anytime.
What if I change my menu seasonally?
Update it as often as you like. The private form takes about two minutes. There's no limit on changes and no extra cost.
Can I add daily specials?
Yes. You can add, edit, or remove items at any time. Many businesses update specials weekly or even daily.
Do I need to be a Gold listing?
The Menu Add-On is available for both Gold and Silver listings. It's included free with Gold and an optional extra for Silver at $199+GST/yr.
Does it replace my own website menu?
No — it complements it. Your listing still links to your website. The interactive menu gives customers an instant preview without navigating away from Papamoa.info.
What about photos of dishes?
Each menu item can have its own photo. Send us your dish photos and we'll add them alongside descriptions and prices. Don't have photos ready? We generate attractive AI placeholder images based on your menu descriptions so customers still get a visual sense of each dish. Swap in real photos anytime through the update form.
