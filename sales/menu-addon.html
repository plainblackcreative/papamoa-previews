Common questions

Frequently asked questions

How quickly can my menu be live?
Send us your current menu (PDF, photo, link, or even a photo of the board on your wall) and we'll have a preview built within 48 hours. You check it, request any changes, and it goes live when you're happy.
When do I pay?
Not until you've seen and approved the preview. We build your menu first, you sign off, then we send the payment link. No surprises.
What if I change my menu seasonally?
Update it as often as you like through your private update form. Takes about two minutes. No limit on changes and no extra cost.
Do I need to be on Gold?
No. The Menu Add-On is available for both Gold and Silver listings. It's included free with Gold and an optional extra for Silver at $199+gst/yr.
Does it replace my own website menu?
No — it complements it. Your listing still links to your website. The interactive menu gives customers an instant preview right on Papamoa.info without navigating away.
What about photos of dishes?
Each item can show a photo. Send us your photos and we'll add them. Don't have photos yet? We generate AI placeholder images from your menu descriptions so customers get a visual from day one. Swap in real photos any time through the update form.
I'm not a food business — does this work for me?
Yes, it's designed for any business with a service list and pricing. Gyms, dental clinics, spas, mechanics, barbers — if you charge for things, a service list helps customers decide before they call. The demo above includes a gym and two dental practices to show you how it looks.
