AI-Assisted Content Disclosure

⚠️
Always verify directly with businesses before visiting. Directory listings, hours, prices and contact details on this site are researched and written with the assistance of AI tools. While every effort is made to ensure accuracy, information can change without notice. Papamoa.info makes no guarantee that any listing detail is current or complete.

How this site's content is produced

Papamoa.info is a local business directory for Pāpāmoa, Bay of Plenty, New Zealand. Business listings, community information, category pages and articles on this site are researched and written using AI-assisted tools, including Claude (Anthropic). Editorial content is reviewed by the site operator before publication, but AI tools play a significant role in drafting, researching and structuring content across the site.

What this means for you

Content produced with AI assistance is based on publicly available information at the time of research. It may include:

  • Business names, addresses, phone numbers and websites sourced from public directories, business websites and online sources
  • Descriptions of products, services and businesses written in a third-party editorial voice
  • Community and local information (transport, health, sports clubs, public services) based on publicly available sources
  • Pricing, hours and availability information that can change at any time without this site being updated

AI-generated content can contain errors, outdated information or inaccuracies. Papamoa.info encourages all visitors to verify directly with businesses and service providers before making decisions based on information found on this site. This is particularly important for: business hours, pricing, availability, medical and health information, and emergency services contact details.

Corrections and updates

If you are a business owner and believe information about your business is inaccurate, or if you are a visitor who has found an error, please contact us at jayden@papamoa.info and we will review and correct it promptly.

NZ Privacy Commissioner guidance on AI

In September 2023, the New Zealand Privacy Commissioner issued guidance on the use of AI tools in relation to the Privacy Act 2020, emphasising transparency, accuracy and accountability. Papamoa.info is committed to those principles and to being open about its use of AI in producing site content.

What personal information we collect

Papamoa.info collects personal information only when you actively submit it to us through a form on this site. We do not passively collect personal information from visitors who simply browse the site.

Information collected through forms may include:

  • Name, business name and trading name
  • Email address and phone number
  • Business address and website URL
  • Social media profile links
  • Messages or enquiries you submit to us
  • Images or files you upload (logos, photos)

Why we collect it

We collect personal information for the following purposes:

  • To process business listing enquiries and registrations
  • To contact you about your listing, any changes, or renewal
  • To publish your business listing on Papamoa.info (with your consent)
  • To communicate about services, pricing or updates relevant to your listing
  • To respond to general enquiries submitted through contact forms

How we collect it

Forms on Papamoa.info use Web3Forms (web3forms.com), a third-party form service. When you submit a form, your information is transmitted to Web3Forms' servers and then forwarded to the site operator's email address. Web3Forms' own privacy policy applies to the transmission and handling of that data — see web3forms.com/privacy.

From 1 May 2026, under new IPP 3A introduced by the Privacy Amendment Act 2025, agencies that collect personal information indirectly are required to notify the individual. If your information is collected or shared in any way other than directly from you, we will take steps to notify you of that collection.

Who has access to your information

Your personal information is accessible to:

  • Jayden Brown / Plain Black Creative — the site operator, for the purposes listed above
  • Web3Forms — as the form processing service (see their privacy policy)
  • Brevo (brevo.com) — email automation platform used for follow-up communications. Brevo's privacy policy applies to any data stored within that system

We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with any other third party for marketing or commercial purposes.

How long we keep it

We retain personal information for as long as your listing is active on Papamoa.info, and for a reasonable period afterwards for record-keeping purposes. If you request deletion of your information, we will remove it from our systems within a reasonable timeframe unless we are legally required to retain it.

Analytics and cookies

Papamoa.info plans to use Google Analytics to understand how the site is used. When analytics is active, Google Analytics will set cookies that collect anonymised browsing data (pages visited, session duration, approximate location). This data does not identify you personally. We will update this policy when analytics goes live.

At present, no analytics tracking is active on this site during the build phase.

Your rights under the Privacy Act 2020

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to:

  • Ask whether we hold personal information about you
  • Request access to that information
  • Request correction of any information that is inaccurate
  • Make a complaint to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner if you believe we have breached the Act

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at jayden@papamoa.info. We will respond within a reasonable time, and in any event within 20 working days as required by the Act.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you can contact the Office of the Privacy Commissioner at privacy.org.nz or on 0800 803 909.
