Bronze clarification: Bronze is an internal label for auto-generated placeholder listings. It is never offered to businesses as a product. Every empty subcategory gets a Bronze placeholder that ranks, captures enquiry leads via Web3Forms, and generates traffic data used as sales ammunition to convert the slot to a paid Gold listing.

Sales Funnel Offers

ℹ
These offers are not visible to site visitors. They exist only within the automated sales funnel. They are not stackable and are not presented as a menu of options.

Standard Funnel Offer — All Qualifying Leads

Included automatically for leads who reach the offer stage of the funnel:

1 Free Silver listing on TaurangaNZ.info included with any paid Papamoa.info listing (Silver or Gold). Doubles backlink value immediately.

Last-Ditch Offer — Selected Leads or Late Funnel Only

Used only when a prospect is hesitating on Gold price. Not offered by default. Either triggered manually for selected leads, or activates at a defined late stage in the automated funnel:

1 "Pay for Silver, get Gold" — Silver price ($599+GST/yr) unlocks the full Gold product including Spotlight position. This offer is the final lever before the prospect drops out.
