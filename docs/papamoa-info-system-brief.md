## 1. MARKET ANALYSIS

### The Opportunity
Papamoa is NZ's largest residential suburb with a current population of approximately 37,800 (June 2024) and projected to exceed 40,000+ by 2028, making it one of the fastest-growing suburbs in the country. Tauranga City overall has around 20,000 registered businesses. Papamoa, representing roughly 24% of the city's population and its entire eastern commercial corridor, conservatively houses 1,500–2,500 active businesses across retail, hospitality, trades, professional services, accommodation, and tourism.

**Target pool for this campaign: 300–600 realistic prospects**, of which 50–100 conversions is achievable at a 10–15% close rate.

### Competitive Landscape
Papamoa.info operates in a category of **one**. No other directory targets Papamoa specifically. Competing directories (Finda, Yellow, BizWin, Cylex, White Pages, Localist, BusinessDistrict) are all national or city-level platforms where Papamoa businesses are buried among thousands of other listings. This is the primary selling point.

| Platform | Geographic Focus | Papamoa Visibility |
|---|---|---|
| Yellow NZ | National | Low |
| Finda | National | Low |
| BizWin NZ | National | Low |
| Cylex | National | Low |
| **Papamoa.info** | **Papamoa only** | **Dominant** |

### Seasonal Advantage
Papamoa draws significant tourist traffic in summer. Businesses in Accommodation, Food & Drink, and Activities have strongest motivation to be listed before peak season. Time this campaign accordingly.

### Revenue Model
- $599 + GST / year = $688.85 NZD per listing
- $1200 + GST / year = $1,380 NZD per listing
- Target mix assumption: 70% standard, 30% premium
- **50 listings = ~$46K–$55K ARR**
- **100 listings = ~$92K–$110K ARR**

## 2. PRODUCT — TIER DIFFERENTIATION

Define the tiers clearly before any outreach begins. Proposed differentiation:

### Standard Listing — $599 + GST / year
- Business name, logo, contact details, address, map pin
- Category placement (Accommodation / Activities / Shops / Services / Food & Drink)
- Business description (up to 300 words, SEO-optimised)

### Premium Listing — $1200 + GST / year
Everything in Standard, plus:
- AI-written, AEO-optimised extended description (500+ words targeting question-based searches and AI answer engines like Perplexity and ChatGPT)
- Photo gallery (up to 10 images)
- Video embed
- Featured/priority placement within category
- Bold or highlighted display in category listings
- Quarterly listing refresh (content updated to stay relevant)
- Monthly traffic report (views, clicks)
- Cross-post to Papamoa.info social accounts
- FAQ section on listing page (structured for AI answer engines)

**Upsell prompt:** Pitch Premium to accommodation, restaurants, and activity providers who depend on discoverability. Standard is positioned as the entry point for service businesses (trades, health, professional).

## 4. LEAD GENERATION SYSTEM

### Step 1 — Scrape Google Maps Business Data (Day 1, 2 hours)

Use **Outscraper.com** to extract all businesses in Papamoa, NZ from Google Maps.

Run these 6 searches in Outscraper:
1. `restaurant papamoa new zealand`
2. `accommodation papamoa new zealand`
3. `shop papamoa new zealand`
4. `tradesperson services papamoa new zealand`
5. `activities tourism papamoa new zealand`
6. `health beauty papamoa new zealand`

**Output per export:** Business name, address, phone, website URL, email (where available), Google rating, category.

Expected yield: 400–700 unique businesses after deduplication.

Cost: ~$10–15 NZD for the full pull.

### Step 2 — Email Enrichment

For businesses without emails in the Outscraper export:
- Upload domain list to **Hunter.io** (free 25/month) or **Apollo.io** (free 50/month)
- For bulk enrichment beyond free limits, use **Snov.io** (free: 50 credits/month)

### Step 3 — Lead Scoring & Segmentation

Import into **HubSpot CRM** (free). Tag each lead with:
- Category (Accommodation / Food & Drink / Activities / Shops / Services)
- Priority tier (Hot / Warm / Cold)
- Contact status

**Hot leads** (contact first): Tourism-facing businesses — accommodation, cafes, restaurants, surf schools, tour operators, holiday parks. These have the clearest ROI case for a listing.

**Warm leads**: Retail, health, beauty, professional services.

**Cold leads**: Trades and B2B — approach later or with a different pitch angle.

## 11. AEO / SEO STRATEGY FOR LISTINGS

Answer Engine Optimisation (AEO) is the practice of structuring content so it surfaces in AI-powered tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google's AI Overview — not just traditional search.

### For Each Listing, Include:
- **Structured FAQ** (3–5 Q&A pairs targeting "near me" and "best X in Papamoa" style queries)
- **Schema markup**: `LocalBusiness`, `GeoCoordinates`, `OpeningHoursSpecification`
- **Natural keyword phrases**: "best café in Papamoa", "things to do in Papamoa Beach", "where to stay in Papamoa NZ"
- **Entity signals**: business name, address, phone, website, category — consistent with Google Business Profile

### Premium Listing Content Prompt Template for Claude:

> "You are writing a premium directory listing for [Business Name], a [category] in Pāpāmoa, New Zealand. Write a 500-word description optimised for both Google and AI answer engines. Include: a compelling opening paragraph, 3–4 service highlights, a 'Who is this for?' section, and a 5-question FAQ that mirrors what a tourist or local would search. Avoid marketing fluff. Tone: helpful, local, trustworthy."

### Domain Authority Play
Encourage all listed businesses to link back to their papamoa.info listing page from their website. This builds domain authority over time and compounds the SEO benefit for all listings.

## 13. ASSUMPTIONS & OPEN QUESTIONS

These need to be confirmed before launch:

1. **Tier features** — the Standard vs Premium split above is proposed. Confirm what your CMS actually supports before publishing these features publicly.
2. **Existing listings** — are there any currently live paid listings on the site? If yes, use them as primary social proof.
3. **GST registration** — confirm whether you are or need to be GST-registered. Stripe can handle GST collection.
4. **Listing creation time** — how long does it take you to publish one listing in the CMS? If it's more than 30 minutes per listing, consider creating a templated workflow or VA role for this.
5. **Who does outreach** — at the volume of 50–100, one person can manage this with the automation above. Above that, a part-time VA for CRM management and email replies is recommended.
