SEO & GEO Page Structure

Updated April 2026

Structural recommendations for Papamoa.info's content expansion. Each page is designed to rank in Google traditional search and appear as a cited source in AI answer engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini — collectively called GEO: Generative Engine Optimisation). Ad spots are noted where they create genuine commercial value. Pages marked ✓ are already built and live in the repo.

SEO (Traditional)

Google blue-link rankings. Requires keyword targeting, internal linking, structured data, fast load times.

Already doing this ✓

AIO / AEO

Google's AI Overviews (formerly SGE). Answer-style content, FAQ schema, clear factual statements, cited sources.

Google-specific subset of GEO

GEO — Use This Term

Generative Engine Optimisation. Covers ALL AI answer engines: ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, Google AIO. Structured facts, local specificity, editorial voice.

✦ Recommended term going forward
