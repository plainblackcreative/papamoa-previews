Named placeholder listings for outreach targets
When actively prospecting a specific business, create a named placeholder for them before making contact — e.g. "Bayline Plumbing" instead of "Plumber Pāpāmoa". Their name is already ranking when they receive the outreach email, which is a much stronger pitch. The "oh that's me" moment drives upgrades. Build named placeholders only for active outreach targets, not as a blanket strategy across all businesses in Pāpāmoa.

Lead gen placeholder listings — own & forward enquiries

Capture enquiries via Web3Forms, forward them to the business for free (goodwill + relationship) or hold as pitch ammunition ("we got 6 plumber enquiries last month — here's what a paid listing looks like"). Doubles as analytics for pricing premium ad spots on article pages. Start with sub-categories where you know real businesses who would benefit. Track forwarded leads in CRM as a warm pipeline.
