Real Estate Agent Outreach

Cold email scripts, follow-up sequence, strategy notes, and pricing reference for the featured agent partnership campaign.

Context

Campaign Overview

ℹ
This is a premium partnership position, not a standard business listing. The real estate featured agent slot is priced and positioned differently from Gold/Silver directory listings. Treat it as a bespoke arrangement, not a product with a public price.

What You Are Selling

  • Exclusive featured position on the real estate category page (one agent, no competitors)
  • Monthly property market blog article targeting Papamoa search terms, published under their name
  • Social media sharing of each article across Papamoa.info channels
  • AI search presence: name woven into locally authoritative content that ChatGPT, Siri, and Google AI draw from
  • Direct leads: no platform between agent and client
  • Compounding value: every article makes the presence stronger

Who to Target

  • Agents with Papamoa-specific focus, not Tauranga-wide generalists
  • Active profiles on RateMyAgent, homes.co.nz, or realestate.co.nz with recent Papamoa sales
  • Independent agents or small boutique agencies tend to be more receptive than large franchise branch managers
  • Look for agents who post their own market content on social, they already understand the value of content
  • Avoid agents at agencies that might see this as competing with their own brand

Pricing Approach

Do not publish pricing on the partner page. This allows for a conversation and avoids sticker shock before value is established. The ask will likely be in the range of $150 to $250/month depending on what the market accepts. Frame it as: "One lead pays for this many times over."

Automation

Follow-Up Sequence

ℹ
This is a manual sequence until Brevo automation is set up for the agent campaign. Track outreach in the CRM. Most responses come on Day 3 or Day 6 follow-ups, not the initial email.
Day 0Initial outreach
Send Email 1 or Email 2 depending on context. Note send date in CRM. Attach partner page PDF if email client.
Day 3First follow-up — light bump
Short one-liner: "Hi [Name], just wanted to make sure my earlier email didn't get lost. Happy to answer any questions or jump on a quick call this week." No new content, no pressure. Just visibility.
Day 6Second follow-up — add value
Link to the live category page or the latest market report. "The category page is live if you'd like to see it in context: [link]. This is where you'd be featured." Show, don't tell.
Day 10Final touch — scarcity close
"I'm going to reach out to a couple of other agents in the area this week so wanted to give you first right of refusal. No hard feelings either way — just didn't want you to miss out if the timing is right." Then move on. No more contact unless they reply.
Sales

Objection Handling

"How do I know it will drive leads?"

You don't, and I won't claim to. What I can say is: this site is being built for long-term local authority, not quick traffic. The value compounds. Your name in front of someone actively searching "real estate agent Papamoa" on Google or asking that question on ChatGPT, that is targeted. One genuine lead covers the cost many times over. The question is whether you want to be the agent who owns this ground before someone else does.

"My agency already has a website and social media."

Great. This isn't a replacement for any of that. It's a second authoritative signal that reinforces your name and links back to your own site. Think of it as a trusted local neighbour recommending you, at scale. It also helps your own website rank better through the backlink from an established local domain.

"What's the price?"

Don't quote a number cold. Respond with: "I'd rather have a quick conversation and make sure it makes sense for your situation first. If it doesn't, I'd rather tell you that than take your money." This builds trust and allows a needs-based price conversation.

"Can I think about it?"

Yes, of course. Give them a clear next step: "Totally fine. I'll follow up in a few days. One thing worth knowing is that I can only work with one agent in real estate, so once this is filled, it's closed. But no rush on my end." Then follow the sequence above.
